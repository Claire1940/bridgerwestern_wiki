<Section icon={Target} title="How to Initiate a Trade">
Trading in Bridger Western is a straightforward process, but it requires players to meet certain criteria, especially for Stands. Follow these steps to successfully trade with another player.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Obtain an Axe">
    First, locate the **lumber NPC** in a house and purchase an axe from him. This tool is essential for gathering Roka, a key trading currency.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Farm Roka (Optional)">
    Head to the forest and chop down trees. There's a chance to obtain a **Roka** from each tree. Roka is used as a trading currency and can also equalize ages during a trade.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Reach Tier 3">
    Ensure your character is at least **Tier 3**. This is a mandatory requirement for trading Stands. Without Tier 3, Stand trades cannot be initiated.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Find a Trading Partner">
    Approach another player you wish to trade with. Make sure they are also ready to trade and meet any necessary requirements.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Initiate Trade with Roka">
    With a **Roka** in your inventory, click on the other player. They will receive a notification asking if they want to trade. Confirm the trade, and the trading interface will appear.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Tier 3 Requirement for Stands">
Remember, you must be Tier 3 to trade Stands. Attempting to trade Stands without meeting this requirement will prevent the trade from going through.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Trading Etiquette and Safety">
Successful trading also relies on good communication and awareness of potential pitfalls. Ensure a smooth and fair exchange by following these guidelines.

### Communicating Your Needs
Clearly state what you are looking for and what you are offering. This helps avoid misunderstandings and speeds up the trading process.

### Verifying Offers
Always double-check the items being offered in the trade window before confirming. Ensure that the correct Stand, Roka amount, or cards are present.

### Avoiding Scams
While the in-game trading system has built-in safeguards, always be cautious of external offers or promises that seem too good to be true. Stick to the official trading interface.
