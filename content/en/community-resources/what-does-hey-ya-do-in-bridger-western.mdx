## The Core Mechanics of Hey Ya

Hey Ya is classified as a legendary, non-humanoid utility stand. Its primary function is to provide a series of "Encouragements"—passive buffs that trigger automatically every few seconds. Unlike stands like Star Platinum or Gold Experience, Hey Ya does not have a traditional move set or physical attack. Instead, it serves as a force multiplier for your existing arsenal of revolvers, rifles, and melee weapons.

The most significant advantage of using Hey Ya is its stamina efficiency. Because it is a passive utility stand, it costs **zero stamina** to keep active. This allows you to maintain your stand while simultaneously utilizing high-stamina gun maneuvers, dodges, and horse-riding techniques without the fear of exhaustion.

However, the frequency of these buffs allows them to stack or cycle quickly, keeping you constantly empowered during a prolonged gunfight.

## How to Obtain Hey Ya in 2026

Getting your hands on Hey Ya requires patience and a bit of luck, fitting for a stand that revolves around RNG. You cannot simply buy Hey Ya from a vendor; you must roll for it using a **Stand Arrow Shard**. 

### Acquisition Methods

There are two primary ways to find the materials needed to roll for Hey Ya:

1.  **Fishing:** This is the most popular method for solo players. By fishing at any river or lake in the frontier, you have a **0.5% chance** to reel in a Stand Arrow Shard. While the odds are low, it is a safe, low-risk way to farm while avoiding the chaos of populated towns.
2.  **Corpse Part Hunting:** Participating in world events and hunting for "Corpse Parts" provides a significantly higher chance of obtaining a Shard. However, these events are often hotspots for PvP, making this a high-risk, high-reward strategy.

Once you have a Stand Arrow Shard, use it to roll for your stand. Because Hey Ya is a Legendary tier pull, you may need to go through several shards before successfully summoning him.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Be wary of other players while fishing or hunting for shards. The 2026 frontier is often filled with "bounty hunters" looking to steal rare drops from unsuspecting fishers.

## FAQ

**Q: Does Hey Ya have any active attacks?**

A: No. Hey Ya is a strictly passive utility stand. It does not have a barrage, heavy punch, or any direct damage moves. Its entire value comes from the randomized buffs it provides to the player.

**Q: Can I use Hey Ya while on a horse?**

A: Yes! One of the best features of Hey Ya is that it stays active while you are mounted. The speed and accuracy buffs can significantly improve your effectiveness in mounted combat.

**Q: Is Hey Ya better than humanoid stands for PvP?**

A: It depends on your playstyle. If you prefer using guns and movement, Hey Ya is excellent. If you prefer close-quarters combat and using stand abilities to combo your opponents, you might find a humanoid stand like Star Platinum more effective.

**Q: What does the blue glow mean when using Hey Ya?**

A: The blue glow indicates that the Auto-Aim buff is active. During this brief window, your bullets will have improved tracking or "snap" to targets more easily, making it the perfect time to fire your weapon.

**Q: How often do the buffs trigger?**

A: Buffs trigger every few seconds. While the exact timing can vary slightly, you can expect a new "Encouragement" message and its associated stat boost roughly every 5 to 10 seconds during active gameplay.
