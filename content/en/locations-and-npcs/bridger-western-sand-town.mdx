<Section icon={Target} title="Early Game Progression in Sandtown">
For newcomers to *Bridger: Western*, establishing a solid financial foundation and acquiring basic gear in and around Sandtown is paramount. Fishing is often cited as the most reliable early-game money-making method, providing the funds needed for essential purchases.

### Initial Steps for Success

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire Fishing Gear">
    Upon spawning, head to the fishing village near Sandtown. Purchase a **fishing rod** and **bait** from the hut. If you're short on Moola, you can earn some by defeating bandits or zombies outside of town areas, which also grant XP and chests.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Fish for Funds">
    Fish near the village or along the river. Fishing provides fish (for selling) and a rare chance for chests containing herbs and stand arrow shards. Engage in skill checks during fishing to maximize your rewards.
    <Callout type="warning" title="Skill Check Alert">
    Failing too many skill checks during fishing will reduce your time and can cause you to lose the chest, so stay focused!
    </Callout>
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Sell Your Catch and Upgrade Your Horse">
    Return to the fishing village hut to sell your fish. Aim to accumulate **2,000-3,000 Bux**. Once you have enough, head to the Horse Stable in Sandtown. Reroll horses until you find one costing **1,000 Bux or more**, prioritizing speed. Legendary or mythical horses are ideal, but even an expensive non-legendary can have good stats (max 1.25 for non-legendary).
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Gear Up in Sandtown">
    With a good horse, you can now safely travel and invest in essential gear. Visit the **General Store** for a **cowboy hat** (absorbs first headshot) and a **poncho** (reduced torso damage). The **Gun Store** is where you'll want to pick up a **Winchester rifle** and a **Whitney Draon** for reliable early combat.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Why Fishing is Key">
Fishing provides a consistent and relatively safe way to earn money without immediately engaging in high-risk combat. The occasional chest can also yield valuable items like Dogbane herbs or arrow shards, crucial for later game progression and Stands.
</Callout>

### Dealing with Encounters and Spawns

While in Sandtown, you're generally safe, but venturing out will lead to encounters. Sandtown also offers strategic spawn points.

*   **NPC Encounters**: Outside of towns, you'll face bandits and zombies. Killing them rewards XP and money chests. Boss zombies can also spawn, offering Dogbane herbs.
*   **Corpse Parts**: These rare event items spawn with a bright beam of light. Picking one up makes you visible, triggering server-wide chases. Holding it long enough can grant you a Stand.
*   **Spawn Points**: The Hotel in Sandtown allows you to set your spawn point, ensuring you respawn in a familiar and safe location after dying. This is invaluable for reducing travel time and regrouping.

<Callout type="info" title="Corpse Part Strategy">
If you acquire a Corpse Part, immediately make for a safe, secluded location or prepare for intense player-vs-player combat. Having a fast horse purchased from Sandtown's stable is crucial for escape or pursuit.
</Callout>
