## How to Pilot Executioner in Real Fights

You can have the perfect loadout and still underperform if your rotation is sloppy. Follow this round structure:

1. **Open with a low-commitment peek**  
   Probe reaction timing before hard committing.

2. **Force one defensive response**  
   Bait movement or panic tool first; don’t dump all damage immediately.

3. **Re-engage with tempo card advantage**  
   Use draw/swap speed windows to retake initiative.

4. **Trigger Executioner pressure at mid-health thresholds**  
   This is where finishing power has best practical value.

5. **Disengage briefly after a failed confirm**  
   Reseting is better than overchasing into counterfire.

### Common Mistakes to Remove

- Overcommitting at full enemy health when your burst window is not ready
- Ignoring defensive cards because “damage should be enough”
- Swapping builds after every loss instead of identifying one repeated mistake
- Running Silver Prodigy without actually maintaining silver bullet usage

> **Tip:** Track your last 10 losses and classify them: aim error, positioning, or build mismatch. Most players blame cards when it’s actually positioning.
