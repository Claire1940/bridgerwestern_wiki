## How to Obtain the Rusty Mare's Leg

Before you can worry about repairing the weapon, you first need to find it. Unlike standard revolvers or repeaters that you can simply purchase at a town gunsmith, the Rusty Mare's Leg is an "RNG" drop obtained through the fishing mechanic. 

To begin your search, you will need to head to a body of water. Most players prefer the docks at **Red Corner** or the areas near the train tracks. Avoid the swamp, as fishing mechanics there are restricted. You will need to purchase a basic fishing rod from the local tackle shop for **150 moola**. 

### The Fishing Process

While you can fish with just a rod, using bait is highly recommended. Bait stays in your hot bar or inventory and doubles the rate at which "bubbles" (fish activity) appear. When the mini-game triggers, you have a small chance of pulling up a chest, an arrow shard, or the coveted Rusty Mare's Leg.

> **💡 Tip:** Always keep bait in your inventory while fishing. It doesn't need to be "equipped" to provide the 2x speed boost to bubble spawns.

## Repairing the Rusty Mare's Leg: Step-by-Step

Once the item is in your inventory, you'll notice you cannot equip it as a functional firearm. This is because the weapon is "rusted" and requires professional restoration. To fix this, you must locate a specific NPC specializing in gun repairs.

### Finding the Repair NPC

The repairman is located in **Ridge County**. If you are coming from a different spawn point, look for the large bridge that connects the territories. 
1. Cross the bridge into the Ridge County side.
2. Look for the NPC standing near the path (often associated with the "can shooting" quests).
3. Ensure you have exactly **1,500 moola** in your balance.

When you interact with him, he will offer to repair the weapon for the 1,500 moola fee. Upon payment, the Rusty Mare's Leg is removed from your inventory, and the restored **Mare's Leg** is sent directly to your bank storage.

### Retrieving Your Weapon

Many players panic when the gun disappears after the repair. Do not worry; it has simply been moved to safety. You must travel to the **City Colony** and enter the bank. Speak to the clerk and select "Open Storage." You will find the shiny, restored Mare's Leg waiting for you there.

## Mare's Leg Stats and Performance

Is it worth the 1,500 moola and the time spent fishing? To answer that, we have to look at how it compares to the Winchester, which is the gold standard for many players in *Bridger Western*. The Mare's Leg functions essentially as a "sawn-off" lever-action rifle. It occupies a unique middle ground between a pistol and a primary rifle.

| Statistic | Mare's Leg | Winchester Rifle |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Body Damage** | ~21 | Higher |
| **Fire Rate** | **Fast** | Moderate |
| **Ammo Capacity** | Lower | Higher |
| **Reload Speed** | Moderate | Moderate |
| **Weight/Handling** | **Excellent** | Average |

The Mare's Leg deals approximately **21 damage** on a body shot. While this is lower than the Winchester, its fire rate is significantly higher. This makes it a formidable choice for players who have a "twitchy" playstyle and prefer to overwhelm their opponents with a volume of fire rather than single, heavy hits.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Because of its lower ammo capacity, you must be disciplined with your reloads. Running out of rounds in a heated duel can be fatal given the lever-action animation.

## Is the Mare's Leg Worth the Grind?

In the current 2026 meta of *Bridger Western*, the Mare's Leg is considered a "niche" but highly effective weapon. It excels in mid-range skirmishes where mobility is key. Because it is technically a shortened rifle, its draw speed is often superior to the heavier long-guns, allowing you to react faster when an enemy jumps you in the wilderness.

However, the **1,500 moola repair cost** is a significant barrier for new players. If you are struggling to keep your wallet full, you might find that a standard revolver or a cheaper rifle serves you better until you've established a steady income through bounties or more fishing.

### Pros of the Mare's Leg:
*   **High Fire Rate:** Faster follow-up shots than most lever-action guns.
*   **Prestige:** Since it requires fishing and a specific quest NPC, it shows other players you've put in the work.
*   **Versatility:** Works well as a secondary weapon for snipers who need something for close-quarters defense.

### Cons of the Mare's Leg:
*   **Costly:** 1,500 moola is a steep price for a weapon that does less damage per shot than the Winchester.
*   **RNG Dependent:** You might fish for hours before seeing a Rusty Mare's Leg.
*   **Low Capacity:** You will find yourself reloading more often than your opponents.

**Q: What does rusty mares leg do bridger western if I am already using a Winchester?**
