**The Dogbane Explained:**
The Dogbane is an exceptionally valuable item from chests. Its versatility makes it highly sought after:
- **Age Reset**: Reduces your character's age by 20 years (minimum age 20).
- **Stand Reset**: Allows you to reset your current Stand, useful if you want to try for a new one or if you already have a Stand and need to clear it for a Corpse Part.
- **High Value Sell**: Can be sold to the Witch for a substantial **2.5k Moola**, making it an excellent source of quick cash.
