## Primary Spawn Locations

Once the server message triggers, the race is on. There are four primary locations where Gyro is known to appear. While he can spawn at any of these points, certain areas have a higher reported frequency based on player heatmaps in 2026. Knowing **where to find gyro bridger western** requires a specific rotation through these zones.

### 1. Ringo’s Cabin
Ringo’s Cabin is widely considered the "primary" spawn point. If the message appears, this should always be your first stop. The cabin is centrally located enough that you can quickly pivot to other locations if he isn't present here. 

### 2. The Swamp Church
The Swamp is a treacherous area, but the abandoned church holds one of Gyro's most common corner spawns. To find this, head toward the Witch's Hut. From the hut, take a sharp right and navigate through the shallow water until the silhouette of the church appears. He usually tucks himself into the corner of the structure to stay out of the elements.

### 3. The Building Near the Swamp
If the church is empty, follow the path leading away from the swamp toward the nearest cluster of buildings. There is a specific standalone structure where he frequently stands right against the exterior wall. This is often missed by players who only check the "major" landmarks.

### 4. Outlaw Hills
The most remote of the spawn points is located in the Outlaw Hills. This area is high-altitude and requires a bit of a trek. Because of its distance from the other three points, many players save this for last. However, if you are already in the northern quadrant of the map when the notification hits, checking the hills first can save you a return trip.
