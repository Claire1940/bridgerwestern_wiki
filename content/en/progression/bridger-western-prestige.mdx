## Getting Started: Choosing Your Path

When you first create your character in 2026, you are faced with a choice that dictates your early game experience: Inlaw or Outlaw. While Inlaws spawn within the safety of established towns, many experienced players recommend the Outlaw path. Choosing the Outlaw Hideout as your starting point places you near several high-value activities, though it comes with the risk of constant conflict.

> **💡 Tip:** New characters no longer spawn with a starter fund to prevent "wipe-farming." Your first priority must be securing a reliable source of income.

Upon spawning, you will be given a basic gun and a mule (press **H** to summon). The mule is slow and has poor stamina, making it a temporary tool rather than a long-term companion. Your first objective is to reach the docks and begin your journey toward the first level of **bridger western prestige**.

## The Bridger Western Prestige and Tier System

Progression in the game is tracked through Tiers. As you gain experience (XP) through fishing, combat, and completing world events, your Tier level will increase. This is the core mechanic of the **bridger western prestige** loop.

| Tier Level | Unlockables & Benefits | Requirements |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Tier 1** | Basic Gun Access, Mule Summoning | Character Creation |
| **Tier 2** | Increased Stamina, Additional Tarot Slot | Earned XP via Fishing/Combat |
| **Tier 3** | Dual Gun Equip (Primary & Secondary), Max Tarot Slots | High XP Threshold |

Reaching Tier 3 is essential for competitive play. It allows you to carry a heavy primary weapon, such as a Tommy gun or a shotgun, alongside a reliable secondary pistol. Furthermore, Tier 3 maximizes your potential to utilize Tarot cards, which provide passive buffs to your reload speed, damage, and movement.

## Money Making: From Fishing to Bounty Hunting

To afford the gear necessary for **bridger western prestige**, you need money. Currently, fishing remains the most consistent and safest method for accumulating wealth in 2026.

### The Fishing Meta
Visit a local vendor to purchase a fishing rod and bait. Head to any body of water and look for the fishing prompts. Not only does this provide "moolah," but it is also a primary source of XP for your Tier progression.

| Item | Sell Value (Approx) | Additional Benefits |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Common Fish** | 10 - 25 Moolah | Quick XP gain |
| **Rare Fish (Snapper)** | 25+ Moolah | High XP gain |
| **Treasure Chest** | Variable | Contains Smoke Bombs, Molotovs, Cash |
| **Arrow Fragment** | N/A | 0.5% Drop Rate; Used for Stands |

When you hook a chest, a mini-game will trigger requiring you to press specific keys (like RT, F, or G) in sequence. Success grants you high-tier consumables and significant cash boosts.

## Weaponry and Combat Essentials

Once you have farmed enough money, head to "Mo's Guns" to upgrade your arsenal. A high **bridger western prestige** rank is useless if you are outgunned in a desert shootout.

### Recommended Loadouts
*   **Primary Weapons:** Rifles for long-range engagements or Tommy guns for close-quarters suppression.
*   **Secondary Weapons:** High-accuracy revolvers for finishing off wounded targets.
*   **Essential Accessory:** The **Cowboy Hat**. It is widely considered the most important purchase as it can protect you from a single headshot, often providing the window needed to return fire.

### Ocular Prowess (The X-Key)
By pressing **X**, you activate Ocular Prowess. This mode is critical for high-level combat. It highlights enemies in your field of vision, improves your overall accuracy, and significantly decreases your reload time. Using this effectively is what separates masters of **bridger western prestige** from casual players.

## Supernatural Progression: Stands and Tarot Cards

The most unique aspect of the game is the inclusion of Stands and Tarot buffs. These elements provide the supernatural edge required for the highest levels of play.

### Tarot Cards
Located in the swamp, a witch offers to read your fortune for 150 moolah. This grants you a Tarot card that buffs your character.
*   **Cost:** 150 for the first card; 50 to reveal alternative options.
*   **Slots:** You unlock more slots as you increase your Tier, with a maximum of three cards at Tier 3.

### Obtaining a Stand
There are two primary ways to obtain a Stand in 2026:
1.  **Corpse Parts:** Random server events will trigger a notification ("A part appears once again") accompanied by a yellow beam in the sky. You must race to the beam, collect the part, and survive while it fuses with your body.
2.  **Arrow Fragments:** A legendary 0.5% drop from fishing. If you are lucky enough to pull one from the depths, you can use it to manifest a Stand immediately.

> **Warning:** Fusing with a corpse part takes time. During this period, you are a high-value target for every other player in the server. Find cover or have allies protect you.

## Transportation and Stability

Your mule will eventually need to be replaced. Better horses offer higher speed, better jump height, and more stamina.

| Horse Type | Price | Max Speed |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Mule** | Free (Starter) | 50 - 60 |
| **Basic Horse** | 150 - 500 | 65 |
| **Elite Horse** | 1,000 - 1,500 | 75 (Max) |

To cross water efficiently, hold **Shift** and the **Jump** key while mounted. This prevents your horse from slowing down significantly in the swamps or rivers.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I check my current Tier for bridger western prestige?**

A: Press the **M** key to open your menu. Your XP bar and current Tier level are displayed at the bottom of the screen.

**Q: What happens if I wipe my character?**

A: Wiping your character allows you to change your appearance and starting faction, but you will lose your current money and Tier progress. This is often done by veteran players to try different "builds" or Stand combinations.

**Q: Is the Cowboy Hat really necessary?**

A: Yes. In the current 2026 meta, the ability to survive a headshot is the difference between life and death. It is the most cost-effective defensive item in the game.

**Q: Can I get a Stand without fighting for a Corpse Part?**

A: Yes, you can fish for an Arrow Fragment, but with a 0.5% chance, it requires significant patience. Most players prefer contesting the yellow beam events for a more guaranteed path to power.
