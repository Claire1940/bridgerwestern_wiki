## Mastering the Basics: Controls and Alignment

Before you can worry about your **bridger western bounty**, you must master the physical mechanics of the game. Bridger Western utilizes a fluid movement system that rewards players who can navigate obstacles while maintaining accuracy.

| Action | Keybind | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **Sprint** | Double-Tap **W** | Increases movement speed and automatically vaults small obstacles. |
| **Combat Roll** | **Q** | A quick dodge maneuver to avoid incoming fire or Stand attacks. |
| **Ocular Prowess** | **X** | Highlights enemies, increases accuracy, and boosts reload speed. |
| **Summon Horse** | **H** | Calls your current mount to your location instantly. |
| **Melee Attack** | **G** | Performs a quick strike with your equipped weapon. |

Upon starting, you are asked to choose an alignment: Outlaw or In-law. While current 2026 mechanics focus heavily on PvP and progression, your alignment sets the tone for your interactions. Outlaws typically find sanctuary in hidden camps, while In-laws frequent established towns like Reachby County.

## The Fishing Economy and Leveling Fast

The fastest way to progress in Bridger Western isn't through constant gunfights, but through the fishing economy. Most veteran players spend their early hours at the Fishing Village to build up "moola" and gain experience points safely.

To get started, follow the train tracks to Reachby County and hop on the train until you reach the forest biome. Locate the **Taco Bait Shop** and speak to the NPC to purchase a fishing rod and bait.

| Fish Type | Average Sell Price | Rarity |
|---|---|---|
| **Cod** | 1,500 Moola | Common |
| **U Bat** | 900 Moola | Uncommon |
| **Special Items** | Varies | Rare (0.5% for Stand Arrows) |

> **💡 Tip:** Fishing is one of the only ways to obtain the Dog's Bane herb, which is required to reduce your character's age. Remember, as you age from dying, your stats may fluctuate, making age management a priority.

## Best Weapons for PvP and Bounty Hunting

Selecting the right firearm is the difference between collecting a **bridger western bounty** and becoming one. The game features a variety of revolvers and rifles, each with distinct recoil patterns and special properties.

### Revolver Tier List
1. **Whitney Dragoon:** The heavy hitter. It boasts the highest damage in the game but suffers from massive recoil and a sluggish reload time. Recommended for experienced marksmen.
2. **Colt:** A tactical choice. Bullets fired from the Colt bounce off surfaces, allowing you to hit targets behind cover or around corners.
3. **Scullfield:** A balanced upgrade from the starter Taurus. It offers higher damage than the base weapon with manageable recoil.

### Rifles and Specialty Weapons
For long-range engagements, the **Winchester** is the gold standard. While the **Mayor's Leg** is an alternative, it requires a tedious "unrusting" quest that costs 1,500 Moola, making the Winchester a more efficient investment for most players. If you prefer a melee-centric build, the **Saber** can be paired with specific cards to parry incoming bullets, though it prevents the use of firearms.

## The Card System: Unlocking Talents

Cards are the "talents" of Bridger Western. You can equip up to three cards as you "Tier Up" (level up). To unlock or reroll your cards, you must visit the **Witch's Hut** located deep within the swamp area.

| Card Name | Effect | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| **Veteran** | Improved bullet tracking/ammo management. | General PvP |
| **Time Heals All Wounds** | Grants passive health regeneration. | Survival/Tank Builds |
| **Evil Eye** | Increases the duration of Ocular Prowess (X). | Snipers |
| **Show Me a Good Time** | +10% Saber damage and bullet parrying. | Melee Builds |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Combining the **High Noon** and **Evil Eye** cards can create a pseudo-"aimbot" effect during Ocular Prowess, but it often comes with a permanent -20% Max HP penalty. Use this glass-cannon build with caution.

## Mastering Horses and Transportation

Your horse is your lifeline. A basic mule is slow and fragile, making you an easy target for anyone hunting a **bridger western bounty**. When buying a horse from the NPC near the Reachby County train station, aim for a mount that costs at least **1,000 Moola**.

Horses with higher price tags have significantly better speed and stamina stats. You can "reroll" the available horses by talking to the NPC until a high-tier mount appears. 

**Horse Tech Tip:** When crossing water, hold **Shift + Space** while mounted. This allows your horse to "gallop" through the water at high speeds, preventing you from becoming a sitting duck in the swamps.

## Mastering the Bridger Western Bounty System and Progression

Progression in 2026 is measured by your "Tier." As you fish, engage in PvP, or complete world events, your Tier will increase, unlocking additional card slots. Because the world is persistent, always remember to **Bank your Moola**. If you are killed while carrying a large sum of cash, you risk losing a significant portion of your hard-earned wealth to your attacker.

If you find yourself being hunted due to a high **bridger western bounty**, use the terrain to your advantage. The verticality of Reachby County and the dense fog of the Swamps are excellent for losing pursuers. Use your Ocular Prowess (X) frequently to ensure no one is crouching in wait around the next bend.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I get a Stand in Bridger Western?**

A: There are two main methods. You can find a Stand Arrow through fishing (0.5% chance) or by collecting Corpse Parts. Corpse Parts spawn every 30 seconds, and the spawn rate increases with more players in the server, though they are highly contested.

**Q: What happens when my character ages?**

A: Every time you die, your age increases. You can see your current age in the menu. If you get too old, you may face stat penalties. Use the Dog's Bane herb, found through fishing or foraging, to reduce your age back to its prime.

**Q: Can I change my cards after I've picked them?**

A: Yes. You must travel to the Witch's Hut in the swamp. The first card reading is free, but subsequent rerolls cost 50 Moola each. This is essential for perfecting your PvP build and managing your **bridger western bounty** status.

**Q: Which gun is best for beginners?**

A: The Scullfield is the most recommended gun for new players. It offers a significant power boost over the starting pistol without the difficult recoil management required by the Whitney Dragoon.
