<Section icon={Shield} title="Risk Management and Troubleshooting">

Using a **bridger western auto fishing** macro is a "use at your own risk" endeavor. Because it is not a fully automated bot, it is less likely to be detected by server-side "movement" checks, but visual detection by other players or moderators remains a threat.

**Common Issues and Fixes:**
- **Missing Overlay**: If you accidentally drag the overlay off-screen, close the macro and delete the `settings.json` file in the folder. Restarting will reset the overlay to the default center position.
- **Inaccurate Clicks**: If the macro presses the wrong keys, your "Tolerance" is likely too high (detecting background pixels) or too low (not seeing the letter clearly).
- **Game Crashes**: Ensure you are not running other intensive background apps that interfere with screen capturing.
