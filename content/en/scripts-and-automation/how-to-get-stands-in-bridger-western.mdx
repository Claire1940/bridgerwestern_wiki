## The Corpse Part Method: High Risk, High Reward

The most direct and fastest way to obtain a Stand is through the Corpse Part system. These items are world-spawn events that signal an immediate "all-out war" across the server. When a Corpse Part spawns, a massive yellow beam of light shoots into the sky, visible to every player on the map.

### How Corpse Part Spawning Works
Corpse parts do not spawn randomly in empty worlds. The game mechanics require a minimum of three players to be present in a server for the timer to begin. Every additional player in the server slightly increases the chance of a spawn occurring every 30 seconds.

| Server Population | Spawn Frequency | Competition Level | Recommendation |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **3-5 Players** | Low | Low | Best for solo players |
| **6-12 Players** | Medium | Moderate | Good for small groups |
| **13+ Players** | High | Extreme | Only for high-skill PvP teams |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Picking up a Corpse Part marks you as a target. If you are hit by another player while the part is merging with you, there is a high chance the part will drop, allowing someone else to steal it.

### Survival Strategy for Corpse Parts
Once you reach the yellow beam and interact with the part, the "merging" process begins. This takes several seconds, during which you are incredibly vulnerable. Expert players recommend having a high-stamina horse parked nearby. As soon as you grab the part, mount up and ride away from the beam to find a secluded area where the merge can finish safely.

## Fishing for Arrow Shards: The Solo Path

If the chaos of PvP isn't your style, fishing offers a peaceful—albeit much slower—alternative for **how to get stands in bridger western**. By fishing in various bodies of water, you have a slim chance of pulling up an **Arrow Shard**.

### Locating the Taco Bait Shop
To start your fishing journey, you must first acquire gear. If you are lost, follow the train tracks across the map. The tracks lead directly to the Taco Bait Shop, which serves as the primary hub for all fishing needs.

| Item | Purpose | Cost |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Fishing Rod** | Required tool to catch items | Standard Gold |
| **Bait** | Increases catch rate | Standard Gold |
| **Arrow Shard** | Grants a random Stand (0.5% drop) | **RNG Only** |

### Fishing Mechanics and Risks
Fishing is straightforward: wait for the blue indicator to move near your bobber, then left-click to reel in. While this is the safest method, the Arrow Shard is a Legendary-tier drop with only a **0.5% chance**. 

> **💡 Tip:** Arrow Shards cannot be banked or stored. If you leave the server or die while holding a shard, it is lost forever. Use it immediately the moment you catch it.

## Obtaining D4C: The Specialized Requirements

Dirty Deeds Done Dirt Cheap (D4C) is one of the most sought-after Stands in Bridger Western due to its unique dimension-hopping abilities. Unlike standard Stands, D4C has specific progression requirements that players must meet in 2026.

1.  **Kill Count:** You must have at least 100 total kills on your record.

3.  **The Desert Ritual:** You must be knocked down in the Desert Biome while a Corpse Part event is active. There is a roughly 10% chance upon being knocked under these conditions that you will receive D4C.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I have more than one Stand at a time in Bridger Western?**

A: No, you can only possess one Stand at a time. If you wish to use a Stand Arrow or merge with a Corpse Part, you must first remove your current Stand.

**Q: How do I find the Taco Bait Shop easily?**

A: The easiest way is to find any train station and follow the tracks. The tracks form a loop that passes directly by the Bait Shop. You can also press 'H' to summon your horse for faster travel.

**Q: What happens if I die while holding a Corpse Part?**

A: If you die or are even heavily damaged during the merging process, the Corpse Part will drop onto the ground. Any other player can then pick it up and start their own merge process.

**Q: Is there a way to change my character's face for free?**

A: Yes! While the shop sells face tools for Robux, you can get a free face change if a player with the **Crazy Diamond** Stand punches you. There is a 10% chance your face will permanently change with each hit.

**Q: Why isn't the Corpse Part spawning in my server?**

A: Ensure there are at least three players in the server. If you are alone, the timer will not run. To optimize **how to get stands in bridger western**, try to find a server with 5-7 players to balance spawn rates with manageable competition.
