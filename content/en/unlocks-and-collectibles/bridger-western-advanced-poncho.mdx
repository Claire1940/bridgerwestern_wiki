## Blueprint Farming Loop (Zero-Moolah Rerolls)

Rerolls are fueled by **poncho blueprints**, and the most efficient known method is fishing chests. This is excellent because it shifts optimization cost away from currency and into repeatable activity.

### Recommended loop

1. Start from the tailor/seamstress area.
2. Ride to the nearest fishing location.
3. Fish until chest drops appear.
4. Open chest(s) and collect blueprints.
5. Return to seamstress and reroll.
6. Repeat until you hit target stat profile.

| Step | Time demand | Resource cost | Output |
|---|---|---|---|
| Travel to fishing spot | Low | Minimal | Setup for chest farming |
| Fishing cycle | Medium | Bait/time | Chest chance + blueprint drops |
| Return and reroll | Low | **0 moolah** | New stat roll on poncho |
| Repeat cycle | Medium | Time only | Better odds of ideal stats |

Because rerolling at the seamstress does not require additional moolah, this loop supports both free-to-play grinders and economy-focused players. The **bridger western advanced poncho** becomes much easier to optimize if you commit to short, repeatable sessions instead of long random marathons.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Don’t spend large amounts upgrading other optional gear before establishing your poncho reroll loop. Early stat consistency is more valuable than scattered small upgrades.

## Best Reroll Strategy and Stat Priorities

Since advanced poncho values are percentage-based, you should reroll with a role in mind. Don’t chase “perfect” without context. Chase **useful** first.

### Build archetypes to target

| Playstyle | Priority stats | Good early stop point | Why this works |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Farm DPS** | Damage %, crit-related bonuses | 1 strong offense roll + 1 useful secondary | Speeds up mob clear and income |
| **Duel/PvP balance** | Damage % + mitigation/survivability | Balanced roll without weak dead stat | Better consistency in trades |
| **Risky PvE routes** | Defense/sustain + utility | Defensive core with one offensive stat | Reduces reset/death downtime |
| **Generalist** | Mixed offense + utility | “No bad stat” roll | Efficient while gearing other systems |

### Practical reroll rules

- Keep a roll if it clearly improves your current bottleneck.
- Replace rolls with dead value for your build (for example, niche utility when you need damage consistency).
- Evaluate performance over 20–30 minutes of normal gameplay, not one lucky fight.
- If your income is low, pause rerolls and farm more blueprints before returning.

The **bridger western advanced poncho** is strongest when you pair it with a clear goal: faster money route, safer boss attempts, or stronger duel pressure. Without that target, players reroll endlessly and stall progression.
