<Section icon={Shield} title="The Aging System and Data Protection">

One of the most controversial mechanics in Bridger: Western is the aging system. Every death increases your character's age by one to two years. Combat logging is penalized even more heavily, adding significant years to your total.

When a character reaches the age of 70, the stakes become absolute. Every subsequent death carries a high probability of a complete data wipe, meaning you lose your Stand, your items, and all progression. To combat this, players must hunt for specific herbs to reverse the aging process.

| Aging Milestone | Effect |
| --- | --- |
| **Age 1-69** | No significant mechanical penalties |
| **Age 70+** | High risk of permanent data wipe upon death |
| **Combat Log** | Massive age increase penalty |
| **Herb Usage** | Reduces age by a fixed amount |

<Callout type="warning" title="Wipe Prevention">
Do not ignore your age. Once you hit 60, prioritize finding herbs. Herbs have a 4% drop rate from chests or can be found spawning naturally in the world.
</Callout>
