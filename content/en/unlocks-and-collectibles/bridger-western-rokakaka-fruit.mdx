## Combat Safety and Mob Spawn Handling

A big pain point in **bridger western rokakaka fruit** farming is random hostile spawn pressure during or right after planting. You don’t need to overfight every time.

### Low-risk response strategy

| Situation | Best Response | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **Zombie/hostile appears at plant** | Create distance first | Prevents panic deaths and item loss |
| **You’re undergeared** | Kite, reset, and return | Safer than forced duel |
| **Multiple mobs stack** | Reposition and break aggro | Preserves harvest timing |
| **Another player pulls mobs to you** | Rotate to a nearby safe line and re-enter | Keeps your cycle alive |

> **Tip:** A short retreat can cause mobs to lose pressure, letting you re-approach and harvest instead of burning consumables in a messy fight.

If your build is strong, clear mobs quickly. If not, your goal is survival and cycle continuity. Dying near harvest timing is usually a bigger loss than a small delay.
