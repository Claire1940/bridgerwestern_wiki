Unlike basic starter clothing, the poncho is a specialized gear item found in the more remote regions of the map. To locate the primary vendor for these items, you must travel deep into the desert biome. Look for a large bridge and a prominent tunnel; these landmarks serve as your gateway to the hidden gear shop.

Once you pass through the tunnel, you will find a small settlement featuring a shop labeled with "Food," "Coyote," and "Gear" signs. This is the central hub for outlaws looking to upgrade their silhouette.

### The Fishing Method
Fishing is the most reliable way for beginners to earn money without risking constant combat. 

1.  **Spawn at Red Corner:** This location is closest to the primary fishing docks.
2.  **Purchase Gear:** Buy a fishing rod and at least one bait from the dock merchant.
3.  **Find a Quiet Spot:** Avoid the main docks if they are crowded with aggressive players. Find a secluded shoreline to cast your line.
4.  **Watch for Bubbles:** When bubbles appear on your lure, click immediately to catch the fish.
5.  **Sell Your Catch:** Return to the merchant to trade your fish for Mula.

## Essential Controls for New Outlaws

To reach the desert shop safely, you must master the movement and combat controls of Bridger: WESTERN. Your horse is your lifeline; without it, you are an easy target in the open desert.

*   **H Key:** Summon your horse to your current location.
*   **N Key:** Mount or dismount your horse.
*   **Shift:** Sprint while on foot or gallop while riding.
*   **F Key:** Perform a horse attack (deals significant damage to players on foot).
*   **X Key:** Activate "ESP" to detect nearby players or hidden threats.
*   **C Key:** Switch to fists if you run out of ammunition during a shootout.

> **⚠️ Warning:** If your horse is killed, it takes approximately two minutes to respawn. Always keep an eye on your horse's health bar during a skirmish.

## Advanced Mechanics: Talents and Stands

Once you have your poncho and a reliable mount, you should focus on your character's power. The "Witch's Hut" is a hidden location in the swamp area where you can spend Mula to "Read your Fortune."

### Talent System
Reading your fortune costs 150 Mula for the first roll. Talents provide passive buffs that can drastically change your playstyle. For example, the **True Cowboy** talent increases your gun damage by 17% while on horseback. You can hold up to three talents as you progress through tiers (levels).

### The Stand System
Bridger: WESTERN features a unique "Stand" system inspired by JoJo's Bizarre Adventure. There are two main ways to obtain a Stand in 2026:
1.  **Corpse Parts:** A golden message will appear saying "It appears once again." This signals a golden beam on the map. If you reach it and hold the part, you have a 15-20% chance every few seconds to manifest a Stand.
2.  **Fishing:** There is a 0.5% chance to pull an Arrow Shard from the water, which can be used to unlock a Stand.

## Character Aging and Permadeath

Your character's age is tracked in the menu (M key). Every time you die, your age increases by 1 to 2 years. If you combat log (exit the game while in a fight), your age increases by a staggering 10 years. As you grow older, your character will develop wrinkles and gray hair. While there is debate in the community about a "max age" resulting in a permanent wipe, it is confirmed that cosmetic aging is a significant part of the immersion.
