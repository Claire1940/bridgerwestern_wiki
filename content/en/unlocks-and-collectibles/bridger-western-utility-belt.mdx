<Section icon={Shield} title="Combat Mechanics and Damage Mitigation">

Understanding how your gear interacts with combat mechanics is the difference between life and death. The "bridger western utility belt" is not a single item, but a philosophy of balancing your defensive stats.

| Defensive Item | Damage Mitigation Type | Weakness |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Cowboy Hat | Negates 1 headshot | Flies off after hit |
| Poncho | Reduces body damage | Increased limb damage |
| Standard Outfit | Neutral | No special bonuses |

<Callout type="warning" title="Important Defensive Note">
While the poncho makes you tankier against center-mass shots, it leaves your limbs exposed. If you find yourself in a shootout with accurate marksmen, prioritize taking cover rather than relying solely on your poncho's damage reduction.
</Callout>

</Section>
