## Best reroll strategy and stat priorities

Rerolling blindly is where players lose momentum. Instead, use a structured system so each blueprint has a purpose.

### Step-by-step reroll framework

1. **Define your role first** (aggressive duelist, general PvE grinder, or balanced).
2. **Set a minimum acceptable roll** before spending any blueprint.
3. **Reroll in batches** (for example, 3–5 blueprints per session).
4. **Stop on meaningful improvement**, not “perfect” luck.
5. **Re-test after gear upgrades** since percentages scale with base stats.

| Playstyle | Priority Focus | Secondary Focus | Reroll Advice |
|---|---|---|---|
| **PvE Grinder** | Consistent damage scaling | Survivability modifiers | Stop early once clears feel smoother |
| **PvP Skirmisher** | Burst/duel pressure | Mobility/sustain | Reroll longer for higher-impact spikes |
| **Balanced Solo** | Reliable all-around bonus | Situational utility | Keep versatile rolls over narrow extremes |

A practical way to evaluate rolls is to track your average time-to-kill on the same enemy group before and after changes. If clear time drops consistently, your roll is working—even if the numbers don’t look flashy.

The **Poncho blueprint Bridger Western** system rewards disciplined rerolling far more than impulsive reroll spam.
