<Section icon={Target} title="How to Spawn the Saint's Heart">
Spawning the Saint's Heart is a multi-step process that deviates from the random nature of other Corpse Part spawns. It requires a communal effort or careful planning to activate, ensuring that the next Corpse Part that appears will be the coveted Heart.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate the Sand Town Pit">
    Travel to Sand Town, a prominent location in the Bridger: Western map. Within Sand Town, there is a specific pit or hole that serves as the activation point for the Saint's Heart.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Gather Ritual Items">
    Collect 3 specific items: **Rokas**, **Dogbanes**, or **Stand Arrows**. Any combination of these three items will work, as long as you have a total of three. These items are generally found throughout the game world.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Fill the Pit">
    Approach the pit in Sand Town and use the 3 collected items (Rokas, Dogbanes, or Stand Arrows) to "fill" it. This action triggers the special spawn condition.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Await the Spawn">
    Once the pit is filled, the **next Corpse Part** that spawns anywhere on the map will be a Saint's Heart. It will appear in one of the regular Corpse Part spawn locations, accompanied by a bright beam of light, just like other parts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Coordination is Key">
Filling the pit often requires teamwork, as gathering three rare items and then waiting for the spawn can be a competitive process. Consider forming a temporary alliance to secure the Heart.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Requirements to Pick Up the Saint's Heart">
Even after successfully spawning the Saint's Heart, simply finding it is not enough. Players must meet a stringent set of personal requirements to be able to pick it up and initiate the merging process. These conditions ensure that only experienced and formidable players can acquire the Heart.

<CardGrid cols={3}>
  <Card title="Killstreak">
    Maintain a current **Killstreak of 15**. This means you must eliminate 15 other players consecutively without dying yourself before attempting to pick up the Heart.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Total Kills">
    Accumulate a minimum of **125 total Kills** throughout your gameplay. This is a cumulative statistic, reflecting your overall combat prowess.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Faction Status">
    You must belong to the **Inlaw faction**. This faction status is crucial, as Outlaws or Neutrals will not be able to interact with the Saint's Heart.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

<Callout type="success" title="Preparation Advice">
