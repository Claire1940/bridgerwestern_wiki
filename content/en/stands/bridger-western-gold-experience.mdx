### Heat Management Strategies

Effective management of the Heat system is what separates a novice from an expert. In Bridger: WESTERN, Heat is generated by landing successful hits and parrying incoming attacks. Once your Heat reaches its maximum threshold, your Stand takes on a golden aura, signaling that your "Lesson" moves will deal maximum stamina damage.

1.  **Aggressive Generation**: Start fights with the Standard Barrage to quickly build Heat.
2.  **Calculated Spending**: Only use Heat-consuming moves like "Heat Manifestation" when you have a clear opening.
3.  **Defensive Recovery**: If your Heat is low, play defensively and use the environment to reset the fight.

## Countering the Gold Experience

While the **bridger western gold experience** is formidable, it is not invincible. The Stand relies heavily on close-to-mid-range combat. If you are facing a Gold Experience user, your best strategy is to maintain distance and use long-range projectiles or "Zones" to keep them from building their Heat meter.

*   **Distance Control**: Use firearms or long-range Stand abilities to chip away at their health.
*   **Stamina Depletion**: Force them to use their "Life Distributor" early to drain their stamina pool.
*   **Environmental Awareness**: Avoid getting backed into corners where their Barrage is most effective.
