## Getting Started: The Lumber Axe

Before you can even think about harvesting rare fruit, you must acquire the proper tools. The primary method for obtaining seeds is through forestry. You cannot simply find these seeds lying on the ground; they are hidden within the timber of the world's trees. To begin, you must head to the Chocolate Bait Shop. 

From the shop entrance, take a right and follow the path toward the Lumber NPC. This character is the gatekeeper to your farming journey. You will need to purchase the Lumber Axe gear for a flat fee of **250 Mula**. While this might seem like a steep investment for a new player, the axe is a permanent addition to your inventory that facilitates both money-making through wood sales and the chance for rare drops.

| Item | Vendor | Cost | Primary Use |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Lumber Axe** | Lumber NPC | **250 Mula** | Cutting trees for wood and seeds |
| **Horse** | Stable Master | Varies | Fast travel between biomes |
| **Storage Box** | General Store | Varies | Saving fruits from loss on death |

> **Warning:** Always ensure you have enough Mula before making the trek to the Lumber NPC, as the walk can be dangerous if you are unarmed and encounter aggressive players or wildlife.

## How to Farm Rokakaka Seeds Fast

Once you have your axe, the real grind begins. Every tree in the game has a small percentage chance to drop a seed when fully cut down. Because this is governed by RNG (Random Number Generation), some players may find a seed in five minutes, while others may spend upwards of thirty minutes swinging their axe.

To maximize your efficiency, you should look for "Double Tree" spots. These are locations where two or more trees are spawned in extremely close proximity. By standing directly between them, you can minimize the time spent walking between nodes. A popular community spot for this is located directly behind the **Outlaw Camp**. This area is secluded enough to avoid most foot traffic but dense enough with timber to allow for rapid-cycle farming.

## Planting and the Greenlands Requirement

Having a seed is only half the battle. To turn that seed into a **rokakaka bridger western** fruit, you must find the correct soil. The game features several distinct biomes, including deserts, swamps, and forests, but the fruit is notoriously picky. It will **only** grow in the **Greenlands**.

To find the Greenlands quickly, head straight from Rich B County. Once you cross the jurisdictional border into the lush, grassy plains, you are in the right place. However, do not simply plant your seed in the middle of a field. Other players can see your growing plant and may attempt to steal the harvest once it matures. 

### Steps for Successful Cultivation:
1. **Find Cover:** Look for a cluster of bushes or a rocky outcropping within the Greenlands to hide your seedling.
2. **Planting:** Select the seed in your inventory and click on the fertile ground. You should see a message stating "Successfully planted the seedling."
3. **The Wait:** It takes approximately **3 to 5 minutes** for the plant to reach full maturity.
4. **Defense:** Stay near your plant. The act of planting often triggers a "mob spawn" event where zombies or other hostile NPCs will appear to attack you.

> **Tip:** Listen for a "breaking glass" sound effect. This audio cue often signals that the plant has reached its final growth stage and is ready for harvest.

## Harvesting and Yield

When the plant is fully grown, it will transform into a vibrant bush with visible red fruits. Clicking on the plant will harvest the fruit and typically yields **three Rokakaka fruits** per seed. This 1:3 ratio makes the farming process highly profitable if you can manage the initial seed drop.

One of the most critical aspects of the **rokakaka bridger western** economy is the risk of loss. In Bridger Western, dying can result in the loss of items in your active inventory. Since seeds are rare and the fruits are valuable for trading, you should never carry more than you intend to use.

| **Seed** | Very High | Plant immediately upon reaching Greenlands |

## FAQ

**Q: Can I plant Rokakaka seeds in the Swamp or Desert biomes?**

A: No. If you attempt to plant a **rokakaka bridger western** seed in any biome other than the Greenlands, the action will fail. You must be within the specific grassy "Greenlands" territory for the seedling to take root.

**Q: Do I lose my Rokakaka fruit if I die?**

A: There is a high risk of losing fruits held in your inventory upon death. It is highly recommended that you store your harvested fruits in a secure storage location or bank immediately after harvesting them to prevent loss to NPCs or other players.

**Q: How many fruits do I get from one seed?**

A: Under normal conditions, a single successfully grown plant will yield three fruits. This makes the farming loop very efficient once you have secured a reliable source of seeds.

**Q: Are the mob spawns guaranteed when I plant a seed?**

A: While not 100% guaranteed, there is a very high probability that hostile NPCs like zombies will spawn near you while the plant is growing. You should be prepared for combat or have a fast horse nearby to flee if the situation becomes too dangerous.
