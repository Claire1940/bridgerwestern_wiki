*   **Play Defensively:** The goal is the streak, not the speed of the kills. It is better to take 30 minutes to get 25 kills than to die at 24.

### Return to Zero (RTZ)
This is the signature move of GER. In [Bridger: Western](https://www.roblox.com/games/), timing is everything. If a Star Platinum or The World user activates Time Stop, you can press **G** to "Return to Zero." This immediately cancels their Time Stop and leaves them vulnerable to a counter-attack. It is the ultimate psychological weapon in PvP.

### The 7-Page Muda
The 7-Page Muda is GER's most feared offensive tool. While it isn't a "true" one-shot against players with maximum health and defense buffs, it deals enough damage to eliminate most players from 70% health or lower. It is best used after stunning an opponent with the Scorpion Shot or during the end of a successful Return to Zero sequence.
