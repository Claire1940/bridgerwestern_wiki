The fastest way to hit the 50,000 XP mark is through consistent NPC farming in high-level zones. Avoid PvP during the early stages of your XP grind to ensure you don't lose your streak or your life prematurely.

Items like the Arrow Shard can be obtained through the fishing mechanic. While tedious, it is a safer alternative to boss hunting if you are solo.

3.  **Stay Mobile:** Never stay in one area for too long, as high-streak players often become targets for the entire server.
