## FAQ

**Q: Can I use the bridger western faux high noon setup if I am playing as a Vampire?**

A: No. The card system is a human-exclusive mechanic. Vampires rely on their innate abilities and cannot purchase or equip cards from Bridgerman.

**Q: Why isn't my auto-aim working even though I have the Blue Card?**

A: The Blue Card provides the auto-aim logic, but it requires the Regular High Noon card to be equipped simultaneously. Check your card slots to ensure both are active. If you only have one slot, you must reach Tier 2 to use both.

**Q: Where exactly is the card shop in the Abandoned Town?**

A: Look for the building with the most intact roof toward the back of the town. The NPC Bridgerman is usually standing in the shadows behind that building, near the card-rolling interface.

**Q: Is the bridger western faux high noon build considered "cheating" by the developers?**

A: No, it is an intended game mechanic. While "auto-aim" sounds like a cheat, it is a legitimate high-tier reward for players who invest in the card system and manage their Moola and Tier progression effectively.

**Q: How much does it cost to get the full setup?**

A: At minimum, it costs 900 Moola (two rolls at 450 each), but because the rolls are random, most players spend between 2,700 and 4,500 Moola before they successfully land both required cards.
