If you are struggling with **how to get d4c in bridger western**, efficiency is key. Standing in the desert and getting shot repeatedly can be draining, especially with the threat of hostile NPCs and other players.

*   **Ammo Conservation:** If you are using an alt account to knock yourself, use the standard revolver. Avoid headshots to prevent accidental full kills, which would reset your 10-kill streak.
*   **NPC Management:** Be wary of zombies or "the dead" that crawl out of the ground. While NPCs are less likely to spawn while you are in active combat with another player, they can still appear and finish you off while you are knocked. 
*   **The Poncho Requirement:** While not required to *get* the Stand, having a Poncho equipped is essential for using D4C's most powerful passive, Dimension Hop.
*   **Server Hopping:** If a server feels "dry" or corpse parts aren't spawning, don't be afraid to switch. However, remember that your kill streak must be maintained during the transition if you're playing on the same character session.
