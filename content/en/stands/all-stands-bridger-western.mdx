## How to Obtain Stands in Bridger Western

The process of acquiring a Stand in Bridger Western is rooted in the classic "Arrow" mechanic. Players must locate or purchase Stand Arrows, which are then used to manifest a spirit based on a randomized roll system. In 2026, the developers introduced several variations of these arrows to help players target specific rarity tiers or types.

To use an arrow, your character must have an empty Stand slot. If you already have a Stand and wish to roll for a new one, you must use a "Rokakaka Fruit" to reset your character's spiritual energy. This creates a loop of farming resources, rolling for spirits, and testing their viability in the open world.

| Item Type | Primary Function | Obtaining Method |
