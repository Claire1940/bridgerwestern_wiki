## Mastering Supreme Domination (Timestop)

The defining feature of **bridger western star platinum the world** is "Supreme Domination." This is the full timestop ability that players have come to fear.

1.  **Preparation:** The user enters a startup animation to stop time.
2.  **Duration:** Time is frozen completely for 6 seconds.
3.  **Range:** The effect covers a 20-meter radius from any angle.
4.  **Risk:** If the user takes damage during the preparation phase, the timestop is cancelled, and the user is stunned for 2 seconds.

To successfully land a timestop in 2026, you should use the **"Reality itself! Bend to my will!"** counter first. This counter allows you to teleport behind an attacker and stun them for 3 seconds, providing the perfect window to safely activate Supreme Domination without the risk of being interrupted.
