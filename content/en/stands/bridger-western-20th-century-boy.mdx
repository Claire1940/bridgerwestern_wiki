Cards represent the passive progression layer of bridger: WESTERN. You earn one card slot per tier, and these cards can drastically alter your combat effectiveness. For a **bridger western 20th century boy** build, you want cards that emphasize durability or movement to complement the Stand's defensive nature.

  <Step num="1" title="Locate the Bridgerman NPC">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Fund Your Rolls">
    Each card roll costs **450 Moola**. Ensure you have a stable farming route before attempting to roll for specific S-tier cards.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Check Your Tier">

  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Synergize with Weapons">

  </Step>
</Steps>
