## What is the Flesh Automaton Card?

Flesh Automaton is a **Legendary** rarity card that functions as a "death-defying" mechanic. In most combat scenarios in Bridger Western, losing your health results in being downed, leaving you vulnerable to a finishing blow. However, with Flesh Automaton equipped, the moment your health drops to the critical threshold, you enter a specialized state known as the "Walking Flesh Bomb."

During this phase, your character begins emitting disturbing, creepy noises, signaling to everyone in the vicinity that an explosion is imminent. After a brief delay of a few seconds, your character detonates, dealing massive area-of-effect (AoE) damage that can instantly wipe out groups of enemies.

| Feature | Detail |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Rarity** | **Legendary** |
| **Trigger Condition** | Health reaching near-zero |
| **Main Effect** | Transformation into a walking bomb |
| **Damage Type** | Massive AoE Explosion |
| **Invincibility** | Immune to Revolvers and Rifles |
| **Duration** | A few seconds before detonation |

> **Warning:** While the explosion is powerful, you are not truly "immortal" during the wind-up phase. High-burst weapons like shotguns can still end your run before you detonate.

## How to Obtain Flesh Automaton

Acquiring this legendary card is a matter of both persistence and "moola" (the in-game currency). Because it is a legendary drop, the chances of rolling it on your first try are slim. You will need to visit specific NPCs to roll for new cards.

### Location and Cost
To get your hands on Flesh Automaton, you must head to the **Abandoned Town** and locate the Card Dealer. Alternatively, you can speak with the NPC **Bridger**. Each roll for a legendary card costs **450 moola**.

1.  **Farm Moola:** Use fishing at the fishing village (near the Taco Bait shop) to quickly amass the thousands of moola required for multiple rolls.
2.  **Locate the Dealer:** Head to the Abandoned Town or find Bridger.
3.  **Roll for Cards:** Spend 450 moola per attempt. 
4.  **Check Rarity:** Look for the legendary glow to signify you have pulled the Flesh Automaton.

## Combat Mechanics: Invincibility and Vulnerabilities

The most significant advantage provided by the Flesh Automaton is the specialized invincibility it grants while you are in the "bomb" state. This allows you to charge directly at your attackers without fear of being picked off by standard long-range weaponry. However, this invincibility is not absolute.

### Damage Resistance Table
Understanding what can and cannot hurt you while the Flesh Automaton is active is vital for survival.

| Weapon/Ability Type | Can it Hurt You? | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Revolvers** | **No** | Completely blocked; no damage or knockback. |
| **Rifles** | **No** | Shots pass through or deal zero damage. |
| **Shotguns** | **Yes** | Can still down or kill you before detonation. |
| **Vampire Abilities** | **Yes** | Bypasses the card's invincibility frames. |
| **Stands** | **Yes** | Stand-based attacks remain a high threat. |

> **Strategy Tip:** If you see an opponent pull out a shotgun or summon a Stand while you are in your bomb state, try to use your movement keys to zig-zag. Your goal is to survive those few seconds until the automatic explosion triggers.

## Progression: Leveling Up for Card Slots

You cannot simply equip every card you find. Bridger Western utilizes a "Tier" system that limits your passive power. To fully utilize a legendary card like Flesh Automaton alongside other buffs, you must level up your character.

*   **Tier 1:** Unlocks your first card slot (Free).
*   **Tier 2:** Unlocks the second slot (Requires leveling and 50 moola).
*   **Tier 3:** The current maximum tier, allowing for three active cards.

To unlock these slots, you must visit the **Witch's Hut** located in the swamp area. Follow the train tracks to Ridge County, then head into the forest biome and look for the hut. The Witch will "read your fortune" to reveal and equip your cards.

| Tier Level | Max Cards | Unlock Location | Cost to Reveal |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Tier 1** | 1 | Witch's Hut | Free |
| **Tier 2** | 2 | Witch's Hut | 50 Moola |
| **Tier 3** | 3 | Witch's Hut | 50 Moola |

## Farming Moola for Your Legendary Rolls

Since each roll costs 450 moola, you will need a reliable income stream. The most consistent method in 2026 is **Fishing**. By visiting the Taco Bait shop and purchasing a fishing rod and bait, you can catch various fish that sell for significant amounts of cash.

*   **Common Fish:** Sell for 50-100 moola.
*   **Rare Fish (e.g., U-Bats):** Can sell for 900+ moola for a stack.
*   **Special Items:** Fishing can also yield Stand Arrows (0.5% chance) and Dog's Bane herbs, which help manage your character's age.

Once you have accumulated roughly 5,000 moola, head back to the card dealer. This budget allows for approximately 11 rolls, giving you a fair chance at hitting the legendary Flesh Automaton.

## Synergizing Flesh Automaton with Other Cards

To become a true menace, you should pair Flesh Automaton with cards that improve your mobility or accuracy before you hit the critical health state. For example, using cards that enhance **Ocular Pro** (activated by pressing X) can help you deal enough damage that your final explosion merely cleans up the remaining survivors.

Popular synergies include:
*   **High Noon + Evil Eye:** Provides enhanced accuracy and a pseudo-aimbot effect during Ocular Pro.
*   **Speed-based Cards:** Helps you close the distance faster once the Flesh Automaton's "Walking Bomb" state is triggered.

## FAQ

**Q: Does Flesh Automaton work against Stands?**

A: No. While the card makes you invincible to standard revolvers and rifles, Stand abilities and attacks can still damage and kill you while you are in the walking bomb state.

**Q: Where is the best place to roll for the bridger western flesh automaton guide cards?**

A: The most common locations are the Card Dealer in the Abandoned Town or by speaking directly to the NPC named Bridger. Ensure you have at least 450 moola per roll.

**Q: Can I stop the explosion once it starts?**

A: No. Once your health hits the trigger point and the creepy noises begin, the explosion is inevitable unless an enemy kills you with a shotgun, vampire ability, or Stand before the timer expires.

**Q: How do I increase my card slots to use Flesh Automaton with other cards?**

A: You must increase your Tier by leveling up and visiting the Witch's Hut in the swamp. You can have a maximum of three cards equipped at Tier 3.
