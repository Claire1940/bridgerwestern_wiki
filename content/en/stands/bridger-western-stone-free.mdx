## How to Obtain the Stone Free Stand

Acquiring a specific Stand in Bridger: WESTERN requires patience and a solid understanding of the progression loop. Unlike basic gear, you cannot simply buy a Stand from a vendor. You must follow the established stand acquisition routes.

1.  **Reach the Leveling Milestone:** You cannot unlock a Stand immediately upon spawning. You must first engage in the opening loop of leveling and Moola farming.
2.  **Locate Stand Arrows or Corpse Parts:** These are the primary items used to manifest a Stand. While the Wiki notes that White Snake and D4C have specific requirements, Stone Free is typically acquired through high-tier Stand Arrows found in rare chests or as rewards for high-level quests.
3.  **The Witch’s Hut and Spin Mechanics:** In the 2026 update, players can visit the Witch in the swamp to reroll talents and refine their Stand's potential. If you are aiming for a specific build, ensure you have enough Moola (at least 450 per roll) to optimize your character's synergy with Stone Free.
4.  **Using the Root Item:** If you manifest a Stand that isn't Stone Free, you will need a **Root** to reset your Stand and try again. Roots are rare drops but essential for players targeting a specific "JoJo" build.

> **💡 Tip:** Always secure your first horse before hunting for rare Stand items. The increased mobility allows you to check multiple chest spawn points across the map much faster.

## Stone Free Moveset and Combat Strategy

The **bridger western stone free** moveset is designed to punish players who stay at a distance. By using string-based pulls, you can drag an opponent into your effective melee range or set them up for a point-blank shotgun blast.

### Move Breakdown

| Move Name | Input | Effect | Strategic Use |

| **Ora Barrage** | [R] | A rapid series of punches dealing moderate damage per hit. | Excellent for finishing off enemies with low HP or breaking guards. |

### PvP Combo: String and Steel

The most effective way to play Stone Free is to treat it as a setup tool for your firearms. Since the game features a ragdoll system, landing a **String Pull** often leaves the enemy vulnerable for a split second.

*   **The "Reeling" Combo:** Use [E] to pull the enemy, immediately swap to the **Hunters Choice** shotgun, and fire. The small spread of the Hunters Choice ensures all pellets hit the target while they are being dragged toward you.
*   **The "Trap & SMG" Combo:** Lay a **String Trap** [T] and bait the enemy into it. Once they are rooted, unleash the **Tommy Gun**. The 45-round magazine will shred their health before the root duration ends.

## Best Card Synergies for Stone Free

As a human character (Outlaw or Inlaw), you have access to the Card system. Each tier you gain allows you to slot one passive card. For a **bridger western stone free** build, you should prioritize cards that enhance your mobility and sustain.

### Recommended Card Build (2026 Meta)

1.  **Winged Man (S-Tier):** Stone Free allows you to stay airborne by swinging from strings. Since Winged Man grants 25% more damage while midair, this is a mandatory pick for aggressive players.
2.  **Free Runner:** This card adds +20 stamina and changes your roll into a slide. Combined with Stone Free’s **Unravel Dash**, you become the most mobile player on the server.
3.  **Time Heals All Wounds:** As a skirmisher, you will take chip damage. Passive health regeneration ensures you don't have to waste Tonics between every encounter.
4.  **Quick Draw:** If you use the pull-into-shotgun combo, Quick Draw adds a 30% damage bonus to your initial shot, often resulting in an instant kill if you hit the head.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Avoid the **Seasoned Hunter** card if you rely heavily on your Stand's health. The 10% reduction in max HP can make you too "squishy" in high-damage Stand battles.
