<Section icon={Sparkles} title="How to Trigger the Evil Laugh Effect">

Activating the decapitation effect and the accompanying audio cue requires a systematic approach to combat. You cannot simply fire wildly and hope to trigger the effect. The system tracks your headshots dynamically throughout the match. Every time you down or kill an opponent with a clean shot to the head, the counter increments. Once you reach the threshold of 40 headshots, the execution system activates.

To achieve this consistently, players must optimize their crosshair placement and understand target hitboxes. The game registers headshots based on the upper collision box of the player model. Because movement speed in the game is relatively fast, tracking targets requires predicting enemy paths rather than reacting to their movements.

| Hitbox Zone | Damage Multiplier | Difficulty Rating |
| Head | 2.5x | High |
| Upper Torso | 1.0x | Medium |
| Lower Torso | 0.8x | Low |
| Limbs | 0.6x | Low |
