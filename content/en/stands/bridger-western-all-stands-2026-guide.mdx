## Getting Started: Controls and Alignment

Before hunting for the most powerful Stands, you must master the basics of movement and combat. When you first spawn, you will choose between being an **Outlaw** or an **In-law**. Currently, this alignment is primarily for roleplay, but it sets the tone for your journey.

| Action | Control Key | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **Sprint** | Double-tap **W** | Increases movement speed and automatically vaults obstacles. |
| **Roll** | **Q** | A quick dodge maneuver to avoid incoming fire. |
| **Summon Horse** | **H** | Calls your current mount to your location. |
| **Ocular Prowess** | **X** | Highlights enemies and increases reload speed/accuracy. |
| **Melee** | **G** | Performs a physical strike with your equipped weapon. |

> **💡 Tip:** Always keep your Ocular Prowess (X) active during fights. It provides a significant accuracy boost and makes it much easier to track targets in dark or cluttered environments.

## How to Obtain Stands in 2026

There are two primary ways to obtain a Stand in Bridger Western: **Stand Arrows (Shards)** and **Corpse Parts**. While Arrows are more accessible for beginners, Corpse Parts are the fastest route for those willing to engage in high-risk PvP.

### Method 1: Fishing for Stand Arrows
The safest way to progress is to head to the **Fishing Village** (follow the train tracks to find it). Purchase a fishing rod and bait from the Taco Bait Shop.
*   **Stand Arrow Chance:** 0.5% chance from any successful catch.
*   **Moola Grind:** Selling fish is the most reliable way to earn money for gear and horses.

### Method 2: The Corpse Part Race
Every 30 seconds, a Corpse Part has a chance to spawn, signaled by a beam of light in the sky. 
1.  **Locate the Beam:** Race to the light source.
2.  **The Merge:** Once you pick up the part, it has a chance to merge with you every few seconds.
3.  **Survival:** You will glow white while holding the part, making you a target for every player on the server. If you die, you drop the part.

### How to Unlock D4C
Unlocking D4C is the ultimate challenge in Bridger Western. You must follow these exact steps:
1.  Accumulate **100 total kills** across your account history.
2.  Achieve a **10-player killstreak** in a single server.
3.  Go to the **Desert biome** and allow yourself to be "knocked" (downed but not dead).
4.  While knocked, a Corpse Part must spawn. There is a **10% RNG chance** for the Heart of the Saint to merge with you, granting D4C.

## The Witch's Hut: Cards and Passives

To truly master your Stand, you must visit the **Witch's Hut** located in the swamp area. Here, you can spend Moola to draw "Cards" that provide passive buffs. You unlock more card slots as you increase your Tier (Max Tier 3).

| Card Name | Effect |
|---|---|
| **Sturdy** | Increases your overall health and resistance. |
| **Desperado** | Boosts damage when your health is low. |
| **Executioner** | Increases damage against players with low HP. |
| **High Noon** | Essential for "Aimbot" builds using Ocular Prowess. |
| **Quick Draw** | Reduces weapon swap and reload times. |

> **Warning:** Every time you die, your character ages. If you get too old, your stats may suffer. Use **Dog Bane Herbs** (found via fishing or rare chests) at the Witch to de-age your character by 20 years.

## Essential Gear and Horse Tips

A Stand alone won't save you in a 2026 gunfight. You need the right hardware. Visit the gun stores in **Ridge County** or the **Outlaw Hideout** to gear up.

*   **Winchester Rifle:** The gold standard for high damage and long-range accuracy.
*   **Whitney Dragon:** A powerful sidearm for mid-range encounters.
*   **Cowboy Hat:** A vital defensive item. It absorbs one headshot completely, falling off instead of letting you take damage.
*   **Poncho:** Reduces torso damage but increases damage taken by limbs.

### Choosing the Right Horse
Don't settle for a cheap mule. Save at least **1,100 Moola** and head to the stables. Reroll your horse until you find one with high **Speed** stats. Speed is the most important stat for escaping Stand users or chasing down Outlaws. You can also use the "Horse Tech" by holding Shift and Space while in water to move significantly faster across rivers.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the best way to get Moola in the bridger western all stands 2026 guide?**

A: Fishing at the Taco Bait Shop is the most consistent and safest way to earn Moola. Selling a full inventory of rare fish like Cod or Batfish can net you over 5,000 Moola in a single session.

**Q: Can I change my Stand once I have one?**

A: Yes. You can visit the Witch in the swamp and pay a fee to reset your current Stand. You will then need to use another Stand Arrow or find a new Corpse Part to get a different one.

**Q: How do I stop aging in Bridger Western?**

A: You cannot stop aging entirely as it happens upon death. However, you can reverse the process by bringing Dog Bane Herbs to the Witch. These herbs are found as rare drops from fishing chests or by defeating zombie bosses.

**Q: Is White Snake obtainable in 2026?**

A: No. As of the current 2026 update, White Snake remains an admin-only specification. Any player claiming to have it is likely an administrator or using exploits.
