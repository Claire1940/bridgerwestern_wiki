<Section icon={Target} title="Decoding the 'Fine Beast' Dialogue and Pricing">

The phrase **"bridger western now that is a fine beast"** isn't just flavor text; it is a specific indicator of the horse's total stat pool. In *Bridger Western*, the game uses a formula that adds up all individual stats (Speed, Stamina, Jump, and Damage) to determine the NPC's descriptive dialogue and the final price tag. 

When the NPC mentions a "Fine Beast" or "Strong legs, clear eyes, a real beauty," you are looking at a horse that likely has a total stat value nearing the current game maximums. These horses typically retail for around **1,400 to 1,500 currency**. While expensive, these mounts offer the most consistent performance for competitive play or long-distance travel across the western map.

| Dialogue Keyword | Stat Implication | Recommendation |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Fine Beast** | Near-max total stats | **Must Buy** if you have funds |
| **Best Runner** | High Speed focus | Great for travel |
| **Strong Legs** | High Stamina / Jump | Good for mountain climbing |
| **Real Beauty** | High overall balance | Solid all-rounder |
| **Mid / Bad** | Low total stats | **Skip** and re-roll |

<Callout type="warning" title="Price vs. Rarity">
Price is determined by the *total sum* of stats. Rarity (Legendary/Mythical) is determined by *individual stats* exceeding the 1.25 cap. It is possible to find a cheap Legendary horse that has overall bad stats but one exceptionally high Jump stat.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Legendary and Mythical Rarity Explained">

The most confusing aspect for new players in *Bridger Western* is the bracket system. Rarity in this game is purely about "stat overflow." Every standard horse has a hard cap of **1.25** on any given stat. To go beyond this limit, you must roll a Legendary or Mythical variant.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Legendary Horses [ ]">
    - **Visual**: Name is in ALL CAPS with single brackets (e.g., `[OLD MOON]`).
    - **Mechanic**: Exactly **one stat** exceeds the 1.25 limit (up to ~1.35).
    - **Value**: High, especially if the boosted stat is Speed.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Mythical Horses [[ ]]">
    - **Visual**: Name is in ALL CAPS with thick double brackets (e.g., `[[LASCAUX]]`).
    - **Mechanic**: Exactly **two stats** exceed the 1.25 limit.
    - **Value**: The absolute peak of horse performance.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

Legendary and Mythical horses also feature unique color palettes that are not available on standard mounts, making them a status symbol within the community. However, remember the "90% rule": while most high-rarity horses are great, the stat boost is random. A Mythical horse boosting Damage and Jump is objectively slower than a standard horse with a 1.25 Speed stat.
