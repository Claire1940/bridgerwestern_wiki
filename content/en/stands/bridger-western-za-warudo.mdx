<Section icon={Swords} title="Core Mechanics and Ability Breakdown">

Understanding the nuances of the **bridger western za warudo** requires mastering the relationship between your stand's skills and your player character's inventory. The stand is not a standalone tool; it is a force multiplier for your existing weaponry.

### The Knife Passive
The defining feature of this stand is the knife synchronization. If you have knives in your inventory and throw them, The World AU will manifest and throw its own set of knives simultaneously. This passive is on a very short internal cooldown, allowing for constant harassment from a distance.

### The Auto-Tracking Chop
The "Chop" ability is more than just a melee hit. Upon landing a chop, the stand applies a debuff to the enemy. For the next 8 seconds, any knives thrown by the player or the stand will auto-track to that target, even if your cursor is not directly on them. This makes TWAU exceptionally dangerous against high-mobility opponents who try to dodge projectiles.

| Skill Type | Tracking | QTE Requirement | Best Use Case |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Standard Chop** | None | No | Close-quarters setup |
| **Tracking Chop** | Auto-homing | Yes | Mid-range execution |
| **Knife Volley** | Passive | No | Constant chip damage |

<Callout type="warning" title="QTE Alert">
The auto-tracking chop often triggers a Quick Time Event (QTE). If you miss the QTE, the follow-up knife throw may miss due to the knockback of the initial hit. Timing is everything.
</Callout>

</Section>

### Gun Synergy in Time Stop
The most "broken" aspect of the TWAU time stop is the damage calculation for firearms. While the stand itself cannot attack during the short TS, the player can use their guns. Each bullet fired during this window deals significantly increased damage upon time resuming.

| Weapon Type | Normal Damage | TS Resumed Damage | Increase % |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Revolver** | ~20-25 | ~40-52 | ~100% |
| **Shotgun** | ~12 (per pellet) | ~40 (per pellet) | ~230% |
| **Knife** | ~15 | ~25 | ~66% |

<Callout type="success" title="Pro Strategy">
During a Short TS, get as close as possible to the enemy's left side and fire a shotgun. The concentrated pellet spread combined with the TS damage multiplier can easily deal 50-70 damage in a single shot.
</Callout>
