## How to Avoid Scams in the Black Market

The unofficial nature of the **bridger western black market** means there is no built-in protection from the developers. To stay safe, veteran traders follow a strict set of community-enforced rules.

### Use a Middleman (MM)
Most reputable Discord servers offer middleman services. A middleman is a trusted moderator who receives the items from both parties and then distributes them once both sides have fulfilled their end of the bargain. Never agree to a high-value trade if the other person refuses to use a verified MM from a known server like Mystic Market or Bridger Market.

### Check the Vouch System
Before trading with an individual, look for their "Vouch" history. A vouch is a public testimonial from a previous customer.
1.  Search the user's ID in the `#vouches` channel.
2.  Look for screenshots of successful trades.
3.  Be wary of "fake vouches" from accounts created on the same day.

### Evaluating L/W Trades
In the **bridger western black market**, you will often see people asking if a trade is an "L" (Loss) or a "W" (Win). Community consultants in servers like *The Resentful* can help you evaluate if your Whitney Dragon is worth the Stand someone is offering you.

> **💡 Tip:** Always record your screen during a trade. If someone "combat logs" or scams you, having video evidence is the only way to get them blacklisted from the community hubs.
