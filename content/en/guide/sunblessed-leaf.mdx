## Brewing Mechanics at the Fireplace

To turn your raw **sunblessed leaf** into a functional consumable, you must interact with a cooking pot near a lit fireplace. This process is not instant and requires the player to remain stationary for a short duration. 

### Step-by-Step Brewing Guide

*   **Step 1:** Ensure you have at least one clean water canteen in your inventory.
*   **Step 2:** Approach a fireplace with an equipped pot.
*   **Step 3:** Open the "Herbalism" menu.
*   **Step 4:** Select the **sunblessed leaf** as your primary ingredient.
*   **Step 5:** Add optional secondary herbs (like Reservoir Root) to create hybrid effects.
*   **Step 6:** Wait for the brewing bar to complete.

> **Warning:** Brewing in the wilderness can attract hostile NPCs or predatory wildlife. Always clear the immediate area before starting a long brewing session.

## FAQ

**Q: Where is the best place to find the sunblessed leaf in Bridger: Western?**
