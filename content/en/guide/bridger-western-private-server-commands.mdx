## Getting Started with Private Servers

Private servers in Bridger Western serve as a sandbox for players to experiment with builds and mechanics. To access the command console, you generally need to be the server owner or have been granted "Admin" status by the owner. Most commands are executed through the in-game chat interface using a specific prefix, typically a colon (:) or a forward slash (/).

### Essential Administrative Commands

Managing a private server requires a set of tools to keep the environment organized. Below is a table of the most common administrative **bridger western private server commands** used to manage players and the server state.

| Command Syntax | Action Performed | Usage Context |
|---|---|---|
| **:kick [player]** | Removes a player from the server | Removing disruptive users |
| **:tp [player1] [player2]** | Teleports player 1 to player 2 | Organizing group fights or trades |
| **:bring [player]** | Teleports a player to your location | Gathering players for events |
| **:announce [message]** | Displays a global message to all players | Server-wide notifications |
| **:time [number]** | Changes the in-game time of day | Testing Ocular Prowess in different lighting |
| **:kill [player]** | Resets the target player character | Testing respawn points or age mechanics |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Excessive use of the `:kill` command will increase a player's age. In Bridger Western, aging occurs every time you die, and you will need Dog’s Bane herbs to reduce your age back to its prime.

## Optimizing Progression in Private Servers

One of the primary reasons players invest in a private server is to avoid the "swarm" effect found in public lobbies. In public servers, obtaining a Stand via corpse parts can be nearly impossible due to high player density. In a private server, the spawn rates for corpse parts are still influenced by player count, but the lack of competition makes it significantly easier to secure your desired Stand.

### The Fishing Strategy
Fishing is the most consistent way to earn "Moola" and level up your Tier. In a private server, you can fish at the Fishing Village or the Taco Bait Shop without the fear of being sniped. 

1.  **Purchase Gear:** Head to the Taco Bait Shop and buy a fishing rod and bait.
2.  **Farm Moola:** Sell your catch back to the NPC. Rare fish can net you upwards of 1.5k Moola per catch.
3.  **Find Rare Items:** Fishing has a 0.5% chance to drop a Stand Arrow, which is a safer (though slower) alternative to hunting corpse parts.

### Horse Management
In your private server, you can take the time to "re-roll" your horse stats. Visit the NPC in Ridge County near the train station. Experts suggest only keeping horses that cost **1k Moola or more**, as these typically have superior speed and stamina stats. Use your private space to test the "Horse Tech"—holding Shift to sprint and Space while in water to navigate rivers at high speeds.

## Advanced Card Customization

Bridger Western features a "Card" system that grants passive buffs. These are unlocked by visiting the Witch's Hut in the swamp area. In a private server, you can freely travel to the swamp and spend your Moola to "Read My Fortune" without being ambushed.

### Top Tier Card Combinations
Using **bridger western private server commands** to give yourself Moola (if enabled by specific admin scripts) allows you to roll for the best passives:

*   **Aimbot Build:** Combining *High Noon*, *Evil Eye*, and *Folk's High Noon* allows you to land shots even when your reticle isn't perfectly centered while using Ocular Prowess.
*   **Healing Build:** Focus on cards like *Secret Technique* or those that enhance the effects of Tonics.
*   **Speed Build:** Look for passives that reduce the stamina drain of sprinting or horse riding.

> **💡 Tip:** You can have a maximum of three cards active at Tier 3. If you get a card you don't like, simply "Reject this Fate" and roll again.

## Weapons and Gear Acquisition

A private server is the perfect place to visit the various gun shops and test the recoil patterns of different firearms. There are two main locations you should frequent:

1.  **Ridge County Gun Shop:** Sells standard pistols, Winchesters, Mavericks, and Tommy Guns.
2.  **Outlaw Hideout:** Located near the Taco Bait Shop. This vendor sells specialized weapons like the Colt and Double Barrel Shotgun, as well as utility items like Steel Wires, Dynamites, and Molotovs.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I enable bridger western private server commands if I'm not the owner?**

A: The server owner must manually grant you admin permissions. This is usually done through a specific owner-only command like `:admin [username]`. Once granted, you can use the standard command prefix to execute actions.

**Q: Can I get Stands faster in a private server?**

A: Yes. While the spawn rate of corpse parts is technically higher in populated servers (spawning every 30 seconds with more players), the lack of competition in a private server means you are guaranteed to get the part once it appears. You can also fish for Stand Arrows with a 0.5% drop rate in total safety.

**Q: Do my stats and Moola save between private and public servers?**

A: Yes, Bridger Western uses a global save system. Any progress you make, including levels, money, horses, and Stands obtained using **bridger western private server commands** or manual farming, will carry over to any server you join.

**Q: What is the fastest way to level up my Tier?**

A: The fastest way to level up is through a combination of fishing and participating in world events. In a private server, you can trigger world events or simply farm fish at the Taco Bait Shop to reach Tier 3 (the max level) in a few hours of dedicated play.
