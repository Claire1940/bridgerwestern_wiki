## Understanding Poncho Stats and Rerolls

The most confusing aspect for many players in 2026 is how the stats are displayed. When you look at your poncho stats, you might see numbers like "5" or "10." It is important to realize that these represent **percentage increases**. 

For example, if your base damage is 50 and your poncho gives you a "10" in damage, your total damage output becomes 55. This scaling makes the poncho increasingly valuable as you acquire better weapons, as the percentage buff applies to your total power.
