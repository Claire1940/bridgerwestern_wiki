What sets this Stand apart from others like Silver Chariot is its crowd control (CC) capabilities. During this window, the enemy is completely immobile for several seconds, allowing you to reposition or set up a devastating follow-up.

Similarly, the **V move (Bubble Volley)** utilizes the bubbles you've gained passively during the fight. While the raw damage might seem lower than other power-type Stands, the utility is unmatched. Blinding an opponent in a game like [Bridger Western](https://www.roblox.com/games/13554033501/bridger-WESTERN) is often a death sentence, as they cannot accurately aim their revolvers or see your next move coming.

### Combat Strategy: The "Stun-Lock" Combo
1. Initiate with **R (Bubble Chop)** to force the enemy to dodge the homing bubbles.
2. Close the distance and land the full **E (Barrage)** to trigger the Sleep effect.
3. While the enemy is sleeping, use **V (Bubble Volley)** to apply Blindness as they wake up.
4. If they try to fire back, immediately activate **G (Spin & Shield)** to deflect their bullets back at them.
