## Combat Changes: King Crimson, Time Mechanics, and Visual Readability

A major gameplay point in **Bridger Western Update 1.6** is the King Crimson rework. In practice, you should expect revised move flow, pressure tools, and altered reactions in active combat. Several players are also reporting timing changes around evasive behavior and time-interaction windows.

## Stars Essence and New Card Buildcraft

For many players, the most important progression system change in **Bridger Western Update 1.6** is **Stars Essence**. It reduces the pain of forced evolution by giving you more control over when and how Star Platinum transitions. That means you can preserve a preferred phase longer and evolve intentionally instead of being pushed by default behavior.

### Why Stars Essence matters

- Greater ownership over build identity
- Cleaner planning around card synergies
- Fewer “accidental power state” moments
- Better onboarding for players learning stand progression

Alongside that, new cards introduce additional conditional power curves. Two notable examples discussed by players are a visibility/ocular-themed card path and an HP-conditional damage modifier.

### Card and progression implications
