## Pre-Farm Setup Checklist (Do This Before Your First Run)

Before you start the leveling loop, spend 10–15 minutes on prep. Most failed farms happen because players skip this part.

### 1) Navigate to the target area
Head toward the tackle store path and locate the tunnel door that leads toward the boxing setup area.

### 2) Set your main spawn
Talk to the nearby NPC and lock your respawn there. This is essential for rapid loop continuity.

### 3) Prepare your alt account
You need your alt in the same game session flow as your main. Players commonly use multi-instance methods so both accounts can run together on one machine.

### 4) Confirm ring interaction order
Your character and alt need to take the correct spots (red/green side workflow) without extra hits or movement errors.

## Optimization Tips for Better bridger western exp farm Results

The method is simple, but efficiency comes from small improvements.

### Server strategy
- Join lower-pop servers when possible.
- Avoid crowded times where players enter the ring area frequently.
- If your run is disrupted 3+ times in 15 minutes, switch server.

### Execution strategy
- Keep movement minimal between trigger points.
- Don’t freestyle inputs; repeat the same action order.
- Use a short break rhythm (every 25–30 minutes) to avoid sloppy mistakes.

### Account strategy
- If possible, set both accounts’ relevant spawn options for tighter loops.
- Keep alt account controls lightweight and predictable.
- Don’t stack extra tasks while farming (chat, trade windows, menus).

> **⚠️ Warning:** Automation tools can seem convenient, but they often break when another player enters your area or event timing shifts. Manual farming is usually more consistent for this specific loop.
