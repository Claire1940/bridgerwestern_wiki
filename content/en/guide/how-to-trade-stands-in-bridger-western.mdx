## Common Challenges and Solutions

Many players find the process of **how to trade stands in bridger western** frustrating due to the high demand and low supply of facilitators. It is common to wait days for a session that fits your timezone.

*   **Timezones:** Facilitators often host sessions around 11 PM to 12 AM EST. If you live in a different region, you may need to adjust your schedule to catch these windows.
*   **Server Lag:** With 25 people in a small area like the bank, lag can occur. Lower your graphics settings to ensure you don't crash during the transfer.
*   **Scammers:** Always check the username of the person holding the Whitesnake stand. If it isn't Westrat or Kyle (or the verified user announced in Discord), do not drop any items or initiate a conversation.

**Q: Is there a fee for trading stands?**

A: Generally, the facilitators do not charge a fee for the service of moving the stands. However, the person you are trading with will likely expect an equivalent value Stand or in-game currency in exchange for their ability.

**Q: What happens if the server is full?**
