## The Rokakku Trading System

One of the most significant additions in the recent update is the formalized trading system. To trade stands with another player, you must utilize the Rokakku fruit. This process is more involved than simply clicking a menu button; it requires active participation in the game world.

### How to Get and Use Rokakku
1.  **Chop Trees:** Use a lumber axe in the forest or swamp areas. Any tree has a chance to drop a Rokakku seed.
2.  **Find Steady Ground:** You cannot plant the seed just anywhere. You must travel to the "Greenlands" or specific grassland areas where the soil is stable.
3.  **Wait for Growth:** Once planted, the tree will grow slowly. It does not make a sound when finished, so you must keep a close watch.
4.  **Harvest:** Pick the Roka from the tree. You now have the currency required for a stand trade.

> **⚠️ Warning:** The Greenlands can be dangerous. It is common for players to get "jumped" by zombies or other players while waiting for their Rokakku to grow.
