## How to Craft Tea: Step-by-Step

Crafting tea is a straightforward process, but it requires you to be at a specific location. You cannot brew these drinks while standing in the middle of a field; you must find a fireplace with a hanging pot.

1.  **Gather Ingredients:** Collect at least one of your desired herbs (e.g., the **bridger western hellfire sprout**).
2.  **Locate a Pot:** Find a fireplace pot, usually found in town hubs or player-made camps.
3.  **Interact with the Pot:** Open the crafting menu and select the herb you wish to brew.
4.  **Consume or Store:** The tea will appear in your inventory. You can drink it immediately or save it for a specific encounter.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Tea effects do not typically stack. Drinking a second tea while one is active will usually overwrite the previous buff, so plan your consumption carefully.

## FAQ

**Q: Where is the best place to find the bridger western hellfire sprout?**

A: The best place to look is the central desert region. Focus on searching the ground near large cacti and rocky ridges, as these are the primary spawn points for high-tier herbs.

**Q: Can I sell the teas I make to other players?**

A: Depending on the current server economy and trading rules, you can often trade rare herbs like the **bridger western hellfire sprout** or the finished teas to other players who don't want to spend time foraging.

**Q: Does the tea effect stay active after I die?**

A: No, in most cases, tea buffs are lost upon death. It is recommended to only consume your tea when you are prepared for a fight or a long journey where you are confident in your survival.

**Q: How long do the herbs take to respawn?**

A: Herb respawn times can vary based on server population, but generally, you can expect nodes to refresh every 15 to 30 minutes. If a spot is empty, try checking a different part of the desert and returning later.
