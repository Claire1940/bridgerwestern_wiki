## Choosing Your Path: Outlaw vs Inlaw

When you first create your character in Bridger Western, the alignment prompt is your first major interaction. You must decide whether you want to live as an Outlaw or an Inlaw. As of the 2026 updates, the alignment system serves as a foundation for your roleplay and potential future faction-based rewards.

| Alignment | Theme | Gameplay Focus |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Outlaw** | Chaos & Rebellion | Thriving in hideouts, banditry, and high-risk PvP. |
| **Inlaw** | Order & Justice | Protection of landmarks, potential bounty hunting, and town security. |

Currently, the mechanical differences between an **outlaw or inlaw bridger western** choice are subtle. The game focuses more on individual progression, such as leveling your "Tier" and acquiring Stands. However, your alignment dictates where you might feel most "at home." Outlaws naturally gravitate toward the Outlaw Hideout located near the swamp, while Inlaws may find Ridgeby County’s main station more aligned with their aesthetic. Regardless of your choice, your primary goal remains the same: survive, earn moola, and grow stronger.

> **💡 Tip:** Don't stress too much over the initial alignment choice. The current game mechanics prioritize your "Tier" level and your Stand/Card build over your faction status.

## The First Steps: Progression and the Fishing Economy

Once you have chosen to be an **outlaw or inlaw bridger western** player and spawned in, your first destination should be Ridgeby County. If you find yourself lost in the vast desert, follow the train tracks. The tracks are the lifeblood of the map and will always lead you to major landmarks.

Most veteran players recommend heading straight to the **Fishing Village** (specifically the Taco Bait Shop). Fishing is the most reliable and "chill" way to level up and earn moola in 2026. 

### How to Start Fishing
1.  **Locate the Taco Bait Shop:** Follow the train until it hits the forest/swamp biome.
2.  **Buy Gear:** Purchase a Fishing Rod and Bait from the NPC.
3.  **Find Water:** Cast your line and wait for a bite.
4.  **Sell Your Catch:** Return to the NPC to sell fish. Rare catches can net you thousands of moola in minutes.

Fishing isn't just for money; it's a gateway to power. There is a 0.5% chance to pull up a **Stand Arrow**, which is required to unlock your JoJo abilities. You can also find "Dog's Bane" herbs while exploring, which are used to reduce your character's age—a mechanic that increases every time you die.

## Transportation: Getting the Best Horse

Walking across the frontier is a death sentence. You need a horse, but not all mounts are created equal. In Ridgeby County, you can find the Horse NPC near the train station.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not buy the cheapest horse available. Low-cost horses have terrible stamina and speed, making you an easy target for snipers.

To get a competitive mount, you should aim for a horse that costs **1,000 moola or more**. You can "re-roll" the horse offered by the NPC by closing the menu and talking to them again. Look for horses with higher base stats to ensure you can outrun bandits.

### Horse Tech: The Water Sprint
A secret trick used by top-tier players involves traveling through water. Normally, water slows you down significantly. However, if you hold **Shift** to sprint and then spam or hold **Space** while on your horse in the water, you can maintain a high velocity, allowing for quick escapes through the swamp.

## Combat Excellence: Guns and the Card System

Whether you are an **outlaw or inlaw bridger western** citizen, you will eventually need to pull your gun. Ridgeby County features a gun shop with standard weapons like the Winchester and Maverick. However, those seeking more specialized hardware should visit the **Outlaw Hideout**.

### Weapon Availability
| Location | Notable Weapons | Special Items |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Ridgeby Gun Shop** | Winchester, Maverick, Tommy Gun | Ammo Packs, Tonics |
| **Outlaw Hideout** | Colt, Double Barrel Shotgun | Dynamite, Molotovs, Steel Wires |

### Unlocking Passives via the Witch
Your "Tier" (level) determines how many Card slots you have. At Tier 3, you can equip three passives. To unlock these, you must visit the **Witch's Hut** in the swamp. 
1.  Pay 50 moola for a "Fortune."
2.  The Witch will grant you a random card (e.g., Secret Technique, Lay of the Tonic).
3.  If you don't like the card, you can "Reject the Fate" and try again.

### The "Aimbot" Build
In the current 2026 meta, certain card combinations are incredibly powerful. By combining cards like **High Noon**, **Evil Eye**, and **Folk's High Noon**, you can essentially grant yourself "aimbot" capabilities while using the **Ocular PRO (X)** ability. This allows you to hit targets even if your crosshair isn't perfectly centered, making you a lethal force in any gunfight.

## Stand Acquisition: Arrows vs. Corpse Parts

Stands are the supernatural manifestations that define Bridger Western's JoJo influence. There are two main ways to get them:

1.  **Fishing (The Safe Route):** As mentioned, there is a very low chance (0.5%) to fish up an arrow. This is time-consuming but safe from PvP.
2.  **Corpse Parts (The Fast Route):** These items spawn every 30 seconds across the map. The more players in a server, the faster they spawn. However, these are highly contested. If you pick one up, expect the entire server to chase you down.

## FAQ

**Q: Does choosing outlaw or inlaw bridger western alignment affect my Stand?**

A: No, your alignment (Outlaw or Inlaw) does not currently restrict which Stand you can acquire or how powerful your abilities are. It is primarily a social and roleplay distinction.

**Q: How do I lower my character's age?**

A: You must find and use the "Dog's Bane" herb. Aging occurs every time your character dies, and reaching a high age can have negative effects on your character's longevity in the world.

**Q: Where is the best place to find Corpse Parts?**

A: Corpse Parts spawn in various locations across the map, often in open areas or near landmarks. Because they spawn every 30 seconds, the best strategy is to find a low-population server if you want to farm them without constant interference.

**Q: What is the maximum Tier level?**

A: Currently, the max Tier is 3. Reaching Tier 3 allows you to unlock all three card slots at the Witch's Hut, which is necessary for creating the most powerful combat builds.
