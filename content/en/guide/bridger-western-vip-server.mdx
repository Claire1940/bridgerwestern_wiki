## The Role of White Snake in the Economy

As mentioned in the reference material, the "White Snake" stand is the "gatekeeper" of the economy. In the lore of the game, White Snake has the ability to "take" and "give" stands, which the developers have cleverly turned into the game's actual trading mechanic. 

When a trade session is announced, you will typically join a server where the White Snake user is stationed at the Richby County bank. They will take your stand "disc" and exchange it with your trading partner. Because there is no automated system, the value of stands is highly subjective and based on community demand. Having a **bridger western vip server** allows you to host "pre-trade" meetings where you can verify that your partner actually has the stand they claim to have before you both attempt to squeeze into a limited-capacity trade server.
