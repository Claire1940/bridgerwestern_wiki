## The Mechanics of Spin Combat

Once you have learned **how to get steel ball in bridger western**, the real challenge begins: mastering the rotation. The Steel Ball operates on a "charge" system. Holding down your attack key increases the RPM (Rotations Per Minute) of the ball, which directly influences the damage and travel speed once released.

### Advanced Maneuvers
*   **The Ricochet:** By aiming at hard surfaces, the Steel Ball can bounce once, allowing you to hit enemies hiding behind cover.
*   **The Curve:** Holding a directional key (A or D) while releasing the ball will cause it to arc, making it difficult for opponents to time their blocks.
*   **Golden Spin:** A high-level technique that consumes the entire Spin meter but results in a massive explosion of force on impact.

> **Warning:** Using the Steel Ball without sufficient stamina will result in a "backfire" animation, leaving you vulnerable to counter-attacks for 2.5 seconds.
