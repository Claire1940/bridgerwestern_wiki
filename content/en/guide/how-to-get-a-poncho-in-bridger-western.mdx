## Locating the Tailor Shop in Ridge County

Finding your way around the sprawling map of Bridger Western can be daunting for new players. To secure your first advanced poncho, you need to head toward the heart of the civilization in Ridge County. The shop you are looking for isn't hidden in a back alley, but it does require you to know which landmarks to look for along the main road.

The Tailor Shop is the primary destination for all your clothing needs. As you travel down the main road, keep an eye out for the distinct architecture of the town center. The shop is located on the left-hand side of the road if you are coming from the main entrance of the county. It is a large, well-marked building that is difficult to miss once you are in the vicinity of the general store and the sheriff's office.

## Meeting the Seamstress NPC

Once you step inside the Tailor Shop, you will find the Seamstress NPC. She is the key to obtaining high-quality gear. Unlike standard clothing items that you might find elsewhere, the Seamstress specializes in "Advanced Ponchos." These are unique items that are tailored specifically for the player upon request.

When you interact with her, she will mention that she can craft brand-new items if you bring her a blueprint. 2.  **Damage Bonus:** Increases the amount of damage you deal with your own weapons.
3.  **Speed Bonus:** Increases your movement speed, allowing for better positioning during gunfights.
