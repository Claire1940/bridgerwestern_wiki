## Advanced Brewing Strategies

First, ensure the fireplace is lit. Stand near the pot and access your crafting menu. It is recommended to brew in batches. Since many of these teas have an expiration or a "freshness" timer once they are placed in your inventory, don't brew more than you can use in a single play session.

> **Warning:** Be mindful of your surroundings while brewing. Fireplaces are often located in public or semi-public areas where you may be vulnerable to ambushes from other players.
