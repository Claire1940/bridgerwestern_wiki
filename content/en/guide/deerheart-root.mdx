## FAQ

**Q: Can I grow deerheart root in my player base?**

A: Yes, but you need an Enchanted Planter and "Soil of the Ancients," which is dropped by forest bosses. Without the correct soil, the seeds will fail to germinate.

**Q: What is the fastest way to farm deerheart root for gold?**

A: The fastest way is to run a circuit through the Ancient Grove during a rainstorm. Ensure you have the Herbalist's Spade equipped to maximize the yield from every node you find.

**Q: Is there a difference between "Fresh" and "Dried" deerheart root?**

A: Yes. Fresh roots are used for alchemy and cooking, while dried roots are used exclusively for leatherworking and smithing. You can dry fresh roots by leaving them in a Drying Rack for 2 hours.

**Q: Why are my harvested roots turning into "Withered Fiber"?**

A: This usually happens if you use a high-damage tool like an axe or a pickaxe to harvest the node. Use a sickle or a spade to ensure the **deerheart root** remains intact during the extraction process.
