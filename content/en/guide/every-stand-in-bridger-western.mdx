## How to Optimize Your Stand Build

Simply having a powerful stand is not enough; you must also invest in the right character stats. In Bridger Western, players earn points that can be allocated to Strength, Agility, and Vitality.

1.  **Strength-Focused Builds:** Best for stands like Star Platinum or The World. This increases the damage of your barrage and heavy hits.
2.  **Agility-Focused Builds:** Essential for stands that rely on movement, such as Silver Chariot or Made in Heaven. This reduces cooldowns on dashes and increases movement speed.
3.  **Vitality-Focused Builds:** Recommended for "tank" stands like D4C or Gold Experience Requiem, allowing you to survive long enough to trigger your counter-abilities.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the rarest stand in the game?**

A: As of the mid-2026 update, stands like **Made in Heaven** and **Tusk Act 4** are considered the rarest due to the multi-stage questlines and low drop rates for the necessary evolution items.

**Q: Can I have more than one stand at a time?**

A: Players typically have one "active" stand slot. However, you can unlock additional storage slots using in-game currency or game passes, allowing you to collect **every stand in bridger western** and swap between them at a Stand Storage NPC.

**Q: How do I get rid of a stand I don't want?**

A: You must consume a **Locacaca Fruit**. This will remove your current stand and allow you to use a new arrow or corpse part. Be careful, as this process is irreversible and will delete any progress made on that specific stand.

**Q: Are there "Shiny" versions of stands?**

A: Yes, there is a very small percentage chance (usually less than 1%) that a stand will appear with a "Shiny" skin. These do not provide a combat advantage but are highly valued in the trading community for their unique visual effects.
