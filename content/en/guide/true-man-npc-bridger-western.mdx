## Combat Guide: Defeating the Duelist

Finding the **true man npc bridger western** is only half the battle. Once he spawns, the "Duel" begins. This is not a standard mob fight; the Duelist uses advanced AI that mimics player movement, including dodging and precise shooting.

### Phase 1: The Standoff
The Duelist will often initiate with a voice line or a specific animation. Do not rush him immediately. He has high initial damage resistance during his "intro" phase. Position yourself near cover, such as the walls of the cabin or nearby trees.

### Phase 2: The Firefight
He uses a high-velocity revolver. In the 2026 patch, his accuracy increases the longer the fight lasts. Your goal should be to end the fight quickly using high-burst damage. If you have already unlocked basic Spin components, use them to stagger him.

### Phase 3: The True Man's Resolve
When his health drops below 25%, he becomes more aggressive. This is where many players fail. He will attempt to close the distance for a finishing blow. Maintain your spacing and focus on headshots to finalize the encounter.

> **💡 Tip:** Use the "Dodge Roll" mechanic frequently. The Duelist's shots are hitscan, but they follow a predictable firing rhythm. Timing your rolls between his shots is the key to survival.
