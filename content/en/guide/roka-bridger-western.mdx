<Section icon={Target} title="Stand Acquisition and Arrow Shards">

To even consider a reset, you first need to understand how to get a Stand. The current meta revolves around the **Arrow Shard**, a rare item confirmed in public gameplay as the primary catalyst for awakening a Stand.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Reach the Level Milestone">
    Most starter guides recommend finishing your first hour of leveling before chasing a Stand. This ensures you have the health and stamina to survive the journey to the acquisition point.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate an Arrow Shard">
    Arrow Shards are found in specific locations across the map. They are highly contested, so bringing a reliable sidearm like the **Colt Ocelot** is advised.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Interact with the Stand System">
    Once the Shard is consumed, you will be granted a random Stand. This is where the **roka bridger western** mechanic becomes relevant if your roll is unsatisfactory.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Evaluate Your Roll">
    Check the Stand's tier. If you land a C-tier Stand but want to compete in high-level duels, you should prepare for a reset immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="S-Tier Targets">
Current community showcases highlight **Silver Chariot**, **Tusk**, and **Hierophant Green** as top-tier options. If you don't get one of these, a reset is often the best long-term strategy.
</Callout>
