**Q: What is the specific button for how to drop items in bridger western console?**

A: On both Xbox and PlayStation, the primary method is to equip the item and hold **D-Pad Down**. Ensure your virtual cursor is disabled for the input to register correctly.

A: No. In the current 2026 version of Bridger: Western, you must dismount your horse before the drop command will function. Attempting to drop items while mounted may result in the item being deleted or the animation glitching.
