When fighting from horseback, remember to use the **F attack (RT on Xbox)**. This allows you to strike enemies without dismounting, keeping your speed high and making you a harder target to hit. If you find yourself outgunned, look for the "Golden Beam" in the sky. This indicates a Corpse Part has spawned—while these are high-value items that can grant massive power, they are also PVP hotspots.
