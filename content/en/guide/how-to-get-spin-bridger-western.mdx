## The Path to Spin: Seven Step Overview

Before you begin your journey, you must understand that obtaining Spin is a linear quest. You cannot skip ahead to the final battle or simply buy the ability with cash. Each objective must be completed in order, and certain requirements—like your character's age—can act as a hard gate to your progress.

## Step 1: Interacting with the Spin Rock

Your first task is to find a Spin Rock. These are specific environmental assets that randomly "charge" with golden energy. There are six known spawn locations across the map. You do not need to find all of them; you simply need to find one that is currently active (glowing with a spiral effect) and walk into it.

| Location | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Canyons** | Near the rocky outcrops in the canyon biome |
| **Dig Site** | Located on a prominent rock near the excavation area |
| **Town** | Take a right at the main town; the spiral is tucked away |
| **The Lake** | Situated on a rock immediately adjacent to the water |
| **Swamp** | Behind the Witch Hut; follow the path straight back |
| **Outlaw Hideout** | Left of the hideout, located on the upper ridge |

If you are struggling to find an active rock, the most efficient method is to **server hop**. Check a known location, and if it isn't glowing, join a different server until you find one that is active. Once you touch the energy, your screen will react, signaling that the first step is complete.

## Step 2: Locating Gyro Zeppeli

Once you have been touched by the Spin energy, you must find Gyro. Much like the rocks, Gyro has six different spawn locations. He is a nomadic NPC, so you may need to check several spots before finding him leaning against a tree or cactus.

*   **Canyon Biome:** Often found standing near the rock formations.
*   **Town Perimeter:** Usually located to the left of the town next to a large cactus.
*   **Inside Town:** He has a rare spawn point within the town limits.
*   **Ringo’s House:** Look near the trees surrounding the house by the swamp.
*   **The Church:** Located inside the church building deep within the swamp.
*   **Outlaw Ridge:** Near the same house area located by the Outlaw Hideout.

When you speak to him, he will tell you that you need to "toughen up your knuckles." This is your cue to head to the Red Corner.

## Step 3: The Red Corner 20-Kill Streak

This is often where players get stuck. You must travel to the **Red Corner Fight Club**. To find it, follow the train tracks starting from the fishing docks until you reach a tunnel. Inside the tunnel is a door leading to the club.

You are required to get a **20 kill streak** within this zone. This means if you die at 19 kills, you must start over from zero. 

> **💡 Tip:** To speed this up, many players use an alt account or enlist a friend. If your "target" is an Outlaw, they can set their spawn at the Outlaw Camp to quickly run back to the tunnel after each death.

## Step 4 & 5: The Duelist’s Mark and the True Man

After securing your 20-kill streak, return to Gyro. He will now task you with obtaining the **Duelist's Mark**. This requires you to defeat the **True Man**, an NPC that spawns with extremely low probability.

The True Man can spawn anywhere that Bandits or Zombies normally appear (such as Ringo's House). He is an RNG-based spawn, meaning there is no guaranteed way to force him to appear. Some players have reported waiting over five hours for a single encounter.

### How to Fight the True Man
When the True Man spawns, a message will briefly appear on your screen telling you to **unequip all tools**. 
1. **Identification:** He looks like a standard player NPC but will stare at you creepily. He despawns after about 30 seconds if not engaged.
2. **Combat Rules:** If you pull out a Stand or a specialized weapon, he will immediately begin firing with perfect accuracy. It is recommended to use basic fists or a standard gun while utilizing cover.
3. **The Reward:** Upon his death, he will say, "Welcome to a true man's world," and you will receive the Duelist's Mark.

## Step 6 & 7: The Final Grind

With the Duelist's Mark in hand, return to Gyro one last time. He will likely tell you that you still need to be "hardened by battle." This refers to your **total kill count**.

*   **100 Total Kills:** You need 100 kills in total across your character's history. These do not need to be a streak, and kills from the Fight Club generally do not count toward this specific total—focus on regular world PvP.
*   **$25,000 Cash:** You must have the funds ready to purchase the ability.

Once you have the kills and the cash, talk to Gyro. If the purchase goes through but you don't see the ability, **reset your character**. You should then have access to the Spin balls.
