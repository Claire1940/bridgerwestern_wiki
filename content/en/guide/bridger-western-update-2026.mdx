## Reworked Abilities: What to Expect from Public White Snake

The current version of White Snake, as demonstrated by staff members like Rico Holiday (Yams), possesses the potent ability to extract an opponent's stand as a physical disc. This disc can then be used by another player, effectively transferring the stand. While incredibly powerful and central to its current utility, this mechanic is deemed too disruptive for widespread public use.

In the **Bridger Western Update 2026**, the publicly released White Snake will feature a modified ability set. Instead of permanently extracting an opponent's stand, the new White Snake will temporarily *disable* it. This means players hit by White Snake will find their stand unusable for a period, preventing them from attacking or using stand abilities in combat. This reworked mechanic maintains the stand's strategic value as a debuffing tool without the extreme consequence of permanent stand loss.

| Feature | Current White Snake (Admin/Staff) | Public White Snake (2026) |
|---|---|---|
| **Obtainment** | Exclusive (Devs, Staff, Trusted) | Obtainable by all players |
| **Primary Ability** | **Extracts** stand as a disc (permanent loss) | **Disables** stand temporarily (combat debuff) |
| **Trading Use** | Essential for player-to-player stand trades | Not intended for trading (official system coming) |
| **Evolution** | No announced evolution | No announced evolution (C-Moon, Made in Heaven) |
| **Rarity** | Extremely rare, inaccessible | Rare, difficult obtainment method |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not expect the publicly available White Snake to function identically to the admin version. Its core purpose and mechanics are being adjusted for game balance and player experience in 2026.

## The Future of Trading in Bridger Western

Currently, White Snake plays an unconventional but critical role in player-to-player stand trading. Since there's no official in-game trading system, players rely on a trusted White Snake user (often a staff member) to extract a stand from one player and transfer it to another. This method, while functional, is cumbersome and relies heavily on intermediaries.

The good news for players is that the **Bridger Western Update 2026** is expected to introduce a dedicated, official trading system. This development will eliminate the need for White Snake to facilitate trades, streamlining the process and making it more secure for the player base. Developers have acknowledged the need for such a system and are working to address underlying economy issues, such as duping and exploits, before its full implementation.

## No Evolution Plans (Yet) for White Snake

Fans of the JoJo's Bizarre Adventure series, from which Bridger Western draws much of its inspiration, often speculate about stand evolutions. White Snake, in the anime, evolves into C-Moon and eventually Made in Heaven, two incredibly powerful forms. However, developers have explicitly stated that as of the **Bridger Western Update 2026**, there are no current plans or announcements regarding any evolutionary forms for White Snake. This means players should not anticipate C-Moon or Made in Heaven being available immediately upon White Snake's public release.

This decision likely stems from a desire to balance the game and introduce new content gradually. Introducing an already powerful stand like White Snake with immediate access to even stronger evolutions could disrupt the game's meta significantly. Future updates might revisit this, but for now, White Snake will stand on its own merits.
