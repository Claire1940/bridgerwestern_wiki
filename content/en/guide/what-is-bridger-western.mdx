## Getting Started: Character Creation and Controls

When you first launch the game, your journey begins with character customization and alignment selection. You can choose to be an **In-law** or an **Outlaw**. While the mechanical differences between these alignments are currently subtle in 2026, they set the stage for your roleplay and future updates regarding world interactions.

The learning curve for Bridger Western can be steep because the game offers very little hand-holding. Mastering the control scheme is the first step toward survival.

| Action | Keybind | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **Sprint** | Double-tap **W** | Increases movement speed and allows auto-vaulting over obstacles. |
| **Roll/Dodge** | **Q** | A quick maneuver to evade incoming gunfire or melee attacks. |
| **Shoot / Aim** | **M1 / M2** | Standard combat controls; right-click to aim down sights for accuracy. |
| **Reload** | **R** | Essential for keeping your firearms ready during a skirmish. |
| **Melee** | **G** | Strike an opponent with your weapon when they get too close. |
| **Ocular Prowess** | **X** | A special mode that highlights enemies and boosts reload speed. |
| **Summon Horse** | **H** | Calls your mount to your current location. |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Be careful when spawning into the world. Bridger Western does not have a "safe zone" for new players; you can potentially spawn directly into an ongoing gunfight.

## The Economy: How to Make Money Fast

In the early game of Bridger Western, cash (or "Moola") is your most valuable resource. You will need it for better horses, more powerful firearms, and essential supplies like tonics. The most reliable and "chill" way to build your bank account is through the fishing system.

To get started, navigate to **Ridgeby County** and follow the train tracks until you reach the **Taco Bait Shop** near the fishing village. Here, you can purchase a basic fishing rod and bait.

| Item | Price | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| **Fishing Rod** | Varies | Used to catch fish and rare items. |
| **Bait** | Low | Increases the frequency of bites. |
| **Dog's Bane** | Rare Drop | A herb used to **reduce your character's age**. |
| **Stand Arrow** | 0.5% Drop | A very rare item that grants a Stand ability. |

Fishing isn't just about the money; it is also a primary way to level up your Tier. As you catch fish like Cod or Bass, you can sell them back to the NPC at the bait shop for massive profits. High-tier players can easily make upwards of 5,000 Moola in a single session by focusing on rare catches.

## Transportation and Horse Tech

Traversing the map on foot is a recipe for disaster. You need a horse to navigate the various biomes efficiently. In Ridgeby County, you can find the Horse NPC near the train station.

When purchasing a horse, do not settle for the cheapest option. The stats of your mount are randomized. It is highly recommended to **re-roll** your horse until you get one valued at **1,000 Moola or more**. These higher-priced horses feature significantly better speed and stamina stats, which are vital for escaping outlaws.

### Advanced Horse Tech
One of the most useful tricks in Bridger Western is "Water Traveling." Usually, water slows you down significantly. However, if you hold **Shift** to sprint and then hold **Space** while on your horse in the water, you can glide across the surface much faster than swimming.

## Combat Mechanics and the Card System

Combat in Bridger Western is more than just clicking on heads. The game utilizes a "Tier" system (Tier 1 to Tier 3) that dictates your power level. As you level up through fishing, PvP, or world events, you unlock **Card Slots**.

To actually view and equip your cards, you must visit the **Witch's Hut** located in the swamp area. 

| Card Type | Effect |
|---|---|
| **High Noon** | Enhances accuracy during Ocular Prowess. |
| **Evil Eye** | Provides tactical information on enemies. |
| **Secret Technique** | Buffs specific movement or defensive stats. |
| **Tonic Secret** | Increases the healing efficiency of tonics. |

> **💡 Tip:** You can create "Aimbot" builds by combining specific cards like **High Noon**, **Evil Eye**, and **Folk's High Noon**. When active with Ocular Prowess (X), your shots will track enemies even if your aim isn't pixel-perfect.

## Unlocking Stands and Corpse Parts

For many players, the answer to **what is Bridger Western** lies in its Stand system. Stands are powerful supernatural entities that fight alongside you. There are two main ways to obtain them:

1.  **Stand Arrows:** These have a 0.5% chance of being pulled up while fishing. This is the safest but slowest method.
2.  **Corpse Parts:** These are high-value items that spawn every 30 seconds across the map. The spawn rate increases with the number of players in the server. However, these are highly contested; expect a massive gunfight whenever a Corpse Part is located.

### The Aging Mechanic
Bridger Western features a unique "Aging" system. Every time your character dies, your age increases. As you grow older, your stats may fluctuate, and eventually, your character may face permanent consequences. To combat this, you must find and consume **Dog's Bane**, a rare herb that reduces your age and keeps you in your physical prime.

## Essential Gear and Where to Find It

To survive the harsh reality of the frontier, you need more than just a gun. You need a full loadout.

*   **Ridgeby County Gun Shop:** Purchase your primary weapons here, including the Winchester, Maverick, and Tommy Gun.
*   **Outlaw Hideout:** Located past the Taco Bait Shop, this hidden vendor sells the **Colt** and **Double Barrel Shotgun**, along with throwables like Dynamite and Molotovs.
*   **General Store:** This is where you buy **Tonics** for healing and accessories like the Lasso, Poncho, and Lantern.

## FAQ

**Q: What is Bridger Western exactly?**

A: Bridger Western is a JoJo's Bizarre Adventure-themed Western game on Roblox. it combines traditional cowboy gunplay with supernatural "Stand" abilities, card-based RPG progression, and an open-world survival loop.

**Q: How do I get my first Stand?**

A: The easiest (but rarest) way is to fish until you find a Stand Arrow. Alternatively, you can hunt for Corpse Parts that spawn in the world, though this usually involves heavy PvP combat against other players.

**Q: Why is my character getting older?**

A: In Bridger Western, your character ages every time you die. To reverse this process, you need to find a rare herb called Dog's Bane, which can be found through exploration or occasionally while fishing.

**Q: How do I unlock more Card slots?**

A: You must increase your Tier level. There are three tiers in total. Once you reach a new tier, visit the Witch in the swamp to "read your fortune" and unlock your new passive cards.
