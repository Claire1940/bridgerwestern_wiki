## Advanced Combat Techniques

Beyond basic moves, there are several advanced techniques that separate the legends from the outlaws.

### Stand Crashing
By landing a heavy attack while an opponent is mid-barrage, you can "Stand Crash" them. This forces their stand to despawn for a few seconds, leaving them vulnerable to gunshots. This is particularly effective against **all stands in bridger western roblox** that rely on constant pressure.

### The "Ghost" Reload
Some stands allow you to reload your firearm while the stand is active and attacking. This "Ghost Reloading" ensures that you never have a moment of downtime, keeping the pressure on your opponent at all times.
