  <Step num="1" title="Understand Your Spawn and Basic Movement">

  <Step num="2" title="Prioritize Leveling and Early Moola">

  <Step num="3" title="Acquire Your First Horse">

### Stand Acquisition and Resetting
Stands are the game's unique power system. Understanding how to acquire and, if necessary, reset them is vital.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Reach Stand Milestone">
    Stands are not available at character creation. You must progress through early leveling to reach the point where Stand acquisition becomes possible.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Identify Stand-Related Items">
    The **Arrow Shard** is a confirmed item linked to Stand acquisition. Keep an eye out for this and other potential catalysts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Explore Multiple Stand Paths">
    Current guides indicate there are "all ways" to acquire Stands, not just one single method. This means exploring different routes and NPC interactions.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Consider Stand Resetting">
    The game allows for Stand resets. This is an important decision that can impact your account planning. Be aware of what you lose or keep before committing to a reset.
  </Step>
</Steps>
