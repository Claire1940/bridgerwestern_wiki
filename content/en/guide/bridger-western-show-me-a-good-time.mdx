When choosing your loadout, consider your Stand. If you have a close-range Stand like Silver Chariot, a **Shotgun** or the **Tommy Gun** provides the necessary cover fire to close the gap. If you have a ranged Stand like Tusk, the **Colt Ocelot** offers the precision needed to pick off targets from afar.

<Section icon={Shield} title="The Card System: Passive Build Optimization">

Cards provide human characters with passive buffs that can drastically alter gameplay. Unlike Stands, which are active entities, Cards work in the background to improve your stamina, health, or damage output. You unlock one card slot for every Tier your character achieves.

**Common Card Effects and Costs:**
- **Roll Cost**: 450 Moola per roll.
- **Location**: The abandoned town, next to the NPC named **Bridgerman**.
- **Restriction**: Cards are for Humans only; Vampires cannot use this system.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Offensive Cards">

    - **Quick Draw**: Faster weapon swap and draw speed.
    - **High Noon**: Massive damage boost during daytime.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Defensive/Utility Cards">
    - **Time Heals All Wounds**: Passive health regeneration.
    - **Sturdy**: +15% maximum health.

  </Card>
</CardGrid>

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Combo">
Pair **Winged Man** with a Stand that has a high-jump ability. You can stay airborne longer while dealing 25% extra damage with your guns, making you nearly impossible to hit for ground-based enemies.
</Callout>
