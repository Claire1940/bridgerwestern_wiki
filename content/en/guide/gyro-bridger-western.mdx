## The "True Man" Quest: Toughening Your Knuckles

Once you meet the XP and cash requirements, Gyro will present you with a challenge. He will tell you to "toughen up your knuckles" before he can teach you the intricacies of the Spin. This isn't just flavor text; it is a direct instruction for a combat trial.

The quest involves defeating a "True Man" or a "True Person" using only your fists. This is often misinterpreted by newer players who try to use their Stands or firearms to complete the objective. To progress, you must engage in melee combat and secure the finishing blow with your knuckles.

### Strategies for the Fist Trial
1. **Target Selection:** Find an NPC or a willing player participant who fits the "True Man" description. 
2. **Health Management:** Since you are restricted to fists, you are at a disadvantage against armed opponents. Use movement and dodging to avoid incoming fire.
3. **The Finishing Blow:** You can soften the target with other weapons, but the final hit must be delivered by your fists for the quest to register.

> **⚠️ Warning:** If you use a Stand ability or a gun for the final blow, the quest will not update, and you will have to find a new target.
