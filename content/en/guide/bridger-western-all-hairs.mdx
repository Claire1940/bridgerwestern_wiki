Lawmen typically opt for groomed, shorter styles. A well-maintained haircut signals discipline and authority. Styles like the "Classic Part" or the "Military Crop" are excellent choices for those wearing a badge.

Outlaws often sport longer, unkempt hair. Since outlaws spend most of their time hiding in the wilderness, a "Wild Man" or "Rugged Ponytail" fits the narrative of someone who doesn't have frequent access to a barber.
