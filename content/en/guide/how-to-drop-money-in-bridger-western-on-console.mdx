To activate the UI selection mode on most consoles, you will need to press the **View Button** (Xbox) or the **Touchpad** (PlayStation). This toggle allows you to move your selection highlight across the screen rather than moving your character's camera.

| Action | Xbox Control | PlayStation Control |

Dropping money physically in the world is often used for quick transactions or "tipping" other players. However, because Bridger Western uses a specific currency system, you must follow these steps to ensure the cash actually leaves your inventory and becomes a pickable item on the ground.

1. **Enable the Cursor:** Press the View Button/Touchpad to free your cursor.
2. **Access the Currency Tab:** Navigate to the top or side menu where your total cash is displayed.
3. **Select the "Drop" Option:** Highlight your cash balance. On console, this usually brings up a sub-menu with "Drop" and "Give" options.
4. **Input the Amount:** A virtual keyboard will appear. Enter the specific amount you wish to discard. 
5. **Confirm the Action:** Press the confirm button on your virtual keyboard. The money will appear as a physical bag or stack of bills at your character's feet.

If you find that the "Drop" button is greyed out, you may be in a "No-Drop Zone" designed to prevent lag in high-traffic areas like the main town square. ## Farming Resources for Wealth Generation

To have money to drop, you first need to earn it. The most consistent way to build wealth in Bridger Western is through resource gathering, specifically by chopping trees in the forest or swamp areas. While chopping, you have a random chance to obtain a **Rokakku Seed**.

### Planting for Profit
Once you obtain a seed, you cannot simply plant it anywhere. The game requires "steady ground." Many players struggle with this on console because the placement indicator can be finicky.
*   **Location:** Head to the Greenlands/Grasslands area.
*   **Placement:** Look for flat, green terrain away from the swamp's mud or the desert's sand.
*   **Growth:** The tree grows silently. Once finished, you can harvest the Rokakku, which is one of the most traded high-value items in the game.

> **Tip:** If you are having trouble finding "steady ground," try the area near the forest entrance. It is widely recognized by the community as the most reliable spot for planting.

## FAQ

**Q: Why can't I see the 'Drop' button on my console screen?**

A: This is usually because the UI cursor is not active. Press the View Button (Xbox) or Touchpad (PS5) to enable the cursor. If it still doesn't appear, you might be in a protected zone where dropping items is disabled.

**Q: Is there a limit to how much money I can drop at once?**

A: Yes, the game often implements a "Drop Cap" to prevent players from accidentally losing their entire fortune or to combat inflation. If you need to transfer a large amount, the Trading Stand is a much more effective method than learning **how to drop money in bridger western on console** via the physical drop command.

**Q: What happens if I drop money and the server crashes?**

A: Unfortunately, any items or cash on the ground are typically lost if a server instance closes. This is why the Trading Stand is the recommended method for 2026, as the transaction is recorded instantly upon both players clicking "Accept."

**Q: Can I drop items like the Rokakku seed directly?**

A: Yes, items can be dropped similarly to money. Select the item in your inventory and look for the "Discard" or "Drop" prompt. Be careful, as items dropped this way can be picked up by anyone in the vicinity.
