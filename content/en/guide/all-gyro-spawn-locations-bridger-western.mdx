## Step-by-Step Obtainment Guide

### Step 1: De-aging Your Character
If your character is over the age of 30, you must find a **Dog Herb**. This item is a rare spawn in the wild or can be obtained through trading. Consuming the herb will lower your age (for example, from 48 down to 28). Gyro requires a "young" body to handle the physical strain of the Golden Ratio.

### Step 2: The Fight Club Grind
Head to the Bandit Hideout and enter the Fight Club pit. You need 15 confirmed wins in this arena. This must be done with your fists.
*   **Pro Tip:** Use the **Sturdy** perk. It provides a 15% Max Health buff, which is invaluable in fistfights where every hit counts.
*   If the Fight Club is empty, consider server hopping or bringing a friend to trade knockouts.

### Step 3: Defeating the True Man
Once your knuckles are toughened, Gyro will mention you lack the "Duelist's Mark." To get this, you must find the **True Man NPC**. He has a random encounter rate, but players have reported significantly higher spawn rates in the **Swamp** biome.
*   Unequip all tools and weapons.
*   Engage him in a fistfight.
*   Winning this fight grants you the Duelist's Mark, signifying you have entered the "True Man's World."

### Step 4: The 100 Kill Requirement
This is the most grueling part of the process. You must have at least 100 player kills on your record. If you are struggling with this, using a high-damage stand like **Purple Haze** in pilot mode can help you rack up kills in crowded areas or the Fight Club. 

## Spin Abilities and Control Modes

Once you have paid the $25,000, you will receive your Steel Balls. The Spin is not just a projectile; it is a versatile tool with four distinct firing modes that can be cycled to suit your tactical needs.

| Mode | Description | Best Used For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Straight** | A high-velocity, long-range linear throw. | Sniping enemies from a distance. |
| **Curving** | Projectiles curve toward the target (soft auto-aim). | Hitting targets behind trees or cover. |
| **Ricochet** | Balls bounce off surfaces and players. | Enclosed spaces or fighting multiple foes. |
| **Drilling** | High-impact shot that deals massive damage (up to 48). | Breaking guards and heavy damage. |

### Advanced Spin Mechanics
*   **Charging:** You can hold the ball to charge the Spin meter on the left of your HUD.
*   **Horseback Passive:** While riding a horse, your Spin charges passively, allowing you to maintain full power while remaining mobile.
*   **Homing (T-Key):** Pressing the 'T' key launches homing steel balls that seek out the nearest threat, dealing consistent damage.

> **💡 Tip:** Using "Curving" mode while on horseback essentially gives you "auto-aim" projectiles. This is incredibly powerful for chasing down bounties or escaping attackers.

## Combat Strategy: Mixing Stands and Spin

The true power of the Spin in Bridger: Western comes from its synergy with Stands. You do not lose your Stand when you acquire the Spin. This allows for devastating combos.
