## The Foundation of Wealth: Fishing for Profit

For many newcomers and solo players, fishing remains the most reliable way to build a bankroll. While it is often considered the "longest" way to progress, it is also the safest. The primary goal of fishing isn't just the fish themselves, but the rare items that can be pulled from the depths, such as Stand Arrows and Shards. These items carry immense value and are the cornerstone of the game's high-end economy.

To begin your career as an angler, you must first locate the **Taco Bait Shop**. If you find yourself lost in the expansive map, simply locate the nearest train tracks. Following these tracks will eventually lead you to a train stop; the Taco Bait Shop is located near one of these major stops. Once there, you can purchase the necessary gear to start your journey.

### How to Fish Efficiently
1.  **Buy Gear:** Interact with the NPC at the Taco Bait Shop to buy a rod and plenty of bait.
2.  **Locate Water:** Find a nearby pier or riverbank.
3.  **The Minigame:** Cast your line. When the blue indicator moves near your character, wait for the bite and left-click to reel it in.
4.  **Sell or Use:** Common fish can be sold for quick cash, while rare pulls should be used to enhance your character's power.

### Professional Tips for Corpse Hunting
To maximize your efficiency in this high-risk environment, follow these professional strategies:
*   **Server Hopping:** Use the server browser to find "low-pop" servers. You need at least three players for a part to spawn, but having only three players significantly reduces the number of people hunting you.
*   **The "Ascend" Trick:** In the server tab, click "Descend" and then "Ascend" to find servers with the minimum required player count.
*   **Evasion is Key:** Once you pick up a part, do not try to fight. Run as fast as possible. If you get hit, there is a high chance you will drop the part, allowing someone else to steal your hard-earned loot.
*   **Merge Immediately:** The goal is to survive until the part merges with you. Once the "Stand" pop-up appears, the item is bound to you and cannot be stolen.
