## The Lumber NPC and Woodcutting Mechanics

To start your journey toward a successful trade, you must first locate the **Lumber NPC**. This character is situated within a small house near the outskirts of the main settlement. Purchasing an axe from him is your first investment in the trading meta. Once equipped, you can venture into the wilderness to harvest the resources needed for trading.

There are two primary locations for woodcutting: the Forest and the Swamp. Each area offers different risks and rewards, and choosing the right one depends on your patience and current goals.

### Forest vs. Swamp Harvesting

| Location | Drop Chances | Chop Speed | Recommendation |

| **Forest** | High chance for **Rokas** | **Fast** | Best for quick trading |
| **Swamp** | Rokas and **Dog Vein Herbs** | **Slow** | Best for multi-resource farming |

> **💡 Tip:** If your primary goal is to get into a **bridger western trading server** and start swapping stands immediately, stick to the Forest. The time-to-kill for trees in the Swamp is significantly higher, which can slow down your farming efficiency by nearly 50%.

## Step-by-Step: How to Initiate a Trade

Once you have your Roka and have confirmed you are Tier 3, follow these steps to trade with another player:

1.  **Locate your partner:** Ensure the other player is also Tier 3.
2.  **Equip the Roka:** Hold the item in your hand.
3.  **Click the Player:** Aim at the player you wish to trade with and click.
4.  **Confirm the Prompt:** A notification will appear asking, "Are you sure you want to trade?"
5.  **Finalize the Exchange:** Both players must accept the confirmation to open the trading interface.

When searching for a **bridger western trading server**, players often look for high-population instances where "trading hubs" have naturally formed. These are usually located near the Lumber NPC or the central town square.

## Understanding What is Tradeable

The trading system in Bridger: Western is unique because it affects character stats in addition to items. When you trade, the game "equalizes" your age. You can monitor your current age by pressing the **M** key to bring up the character menu. This is particularly useful for players who want to sync their character progression with their teammates.

## FAQ

**Q: Why can't I trade my Roka with another player?**

A: The most common reason is that either you or the person you are trading with has not reached **Tier 3**. Additionally, ensure you are clicking directly on the player model while the Roka is equipped.

**Q: Does every stand get a skin during a trade?**

A: No. There is only a **1% chance** to grant a skin to a stand that doesn't already have one. Furthermore, certain stands like **Golden Experience Requiem** and **Tusk** do not have skins available in the current 2026 update.

**Q: Where is the best place to find a bridger western trading server?**

A: Most players congregate in the main town near the Lumber NPC's house. For more organized trading, many users join community Discord servers where they can coordinate specific item swaps before joining a game instance.

**Q: Is the Swamp better than the Forest for farming?**

A: For Rokas specifically, the Forest is superior due to the faster chopping speed. However, the Swamp is the only place to find **Dog Vein Herbs**, so it is better for players who need multiple types of crafting materials.
