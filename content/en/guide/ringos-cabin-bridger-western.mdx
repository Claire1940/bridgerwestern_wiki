## Mastering the 2026 Trading Update

The trading system in Bridger: Western has seen a massive overhaul. Trading is no longer just about swapping items; it involves your character's age, cards, and most importantly, your Stands. To participate in this economy, you must reach **Tier Three**. Attempting to trade before reaching this tier will result in a failed transaction, so focus on your progression first.

To initiate a trade, you must have a Roka in your inventory. By clicking on another player while holding the Roka, you send a trade notification. This process "equalizes your age," which can be monitored by pressing the 'M' key to open your menu.
