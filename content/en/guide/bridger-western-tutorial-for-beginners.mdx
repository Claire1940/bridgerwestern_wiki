## Basic Controls and Initial Steps

Before you can start hunting for Corpse Parts, you need to master the movement and combat basics. Bridger Western uses a standard third-person control scheme with several unique additions that are vital for both exploration and PvP.

| Command | Action | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Double Tap W** | Sprint | Essential for navigating between towns; automatically vaults obstacles. |
| **Q Key** | Roll | Provides a brief window of invulnerability and repositioning. |
| **M1 / Right Click** | Shoot / Aim | Standard combat controls for all firearms and bows. |
| **R Key** | Reload | Manually reload your weapon; speed can be buffed by cards. |
| **X Key** | Ocular Prowess | Highlights enemies and improves accuracy and reload speed. |
| **H Key** | Summon Horse | Calls your active horse to your location instantly. |
| **G Key** | Melee | Performs a physical strike with your equipped weapon. |

Upon spawning, your first objective should be to find the train tracks. These tracks act as the primary navigation tool for the map, leading you to major hubs like Ridgeby County and the Fishing Village. Following the tracks is the safest way for a new player to find their bearings without getting lost in the vast desert or swamp biomes.

## The Fishing Economy: Earning Moola Fast

For any **bridger western tutorial for beginners**, the most consistent advice is to head straight to the Fishing Village. Combat with bandits and zombies can be lucrative, but fishing is the safest and most efficient way to build your initial bankroll, known as Moola.

1.  **Locate the Taco Bait Shop:** Follow the train tracks until you reach the bridge near the Fishing Village.
2.  **Purchase Gear:** Talk to the NPC inside the hut to buy a fishing rod and bait.
3.  **Start the Mini-game:** Stand near the water and cast your line. When a fish bites, you must complete a skill check by pressing specific keys within a time limit.
4.  **Sell Your Catch:** Return to the NPC to sell your fish. Rare catches can net you thousands of Moola in a single session.

> **💡 Tip:** While fishing, you have a 0.5% chance to pull up a Stand Arrow or Shards. This is a "peaceful" way to obtain your first Stand without engaging in dangerous PvP over Corpse Parts.

## Essential Gear and Ridgeby County Services

Once you have accumulated a few thousand Moola, head to Ridgeby County. This town serves as the main social and commercial hub. You should prioritize buying defensive gear immediately to increase your survivability.

| Item | Location | Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Cowboy Hat** | General Store | Absorbs **one headshot** for zero damage before breaking. |
| **Poncho** | General Store | Reduces damage to the **torso** but increases limb damage. |
| **Tonic** | Doctor/Store | The primary method for **healing health** during or after combat. |
| **Winchester** | Gun Store | A high-damage, accurate rifle recommended for all beginners. |
| **Whitney Dragon**| Gun Store | A reliable sidearm that balances fire rate and stopping power. |

Ridgeby County also houses the **Bank**, where you can store weapons and items. Since there is a limit on how much you can carry, use the bank to keep your rare loot safe from being lost upon death. Additionally, visit the **Barber** and **Tailor** to customize your appearance, though these are purely cosmetic.

## How to Get Stands and Corpse Parts

Stands are the supernatural manifestations that provide unique abilities. In Bridger Western, there are two primary ways to obtain them: Stand Arrows (from fishing) and Corpse Parts.

### The Corpse Part Cycle
Every so often, a server-wide message will announce that a Corpse Part has spawned. A beam of light will appear in the sky, marking its location. 
*   **The Glow:** If you pick up a Corpse Part, your character will glow white. Every player on the map can see this glow, making you a target.
*   **Merging:** While holding the part, there is a chance every few seconds that it will merge with you, granting you a random Stand.
*   **Dropping:** If you are killed while holding the part, it drops for someone else to claim.

### Stand Tier Overview
While most Stands are obtainable through luck, some require specific actions.

*   **Common/Rare Stands:** Star Platinum, The World, Silver Chariot, Crazy Diamond, etc.
*   **D4C (Dirty Deeds Done Dirt Cheap):** This is the hardest Stand to obtain. You need 100 total kills, a 10-kill streak in a single server, and a 10% RNG chance while being "knocked" in the desert when a Corpse Part spawns.
*   **White Snake:** Currently an admin-only Stand and not obtainable by regular players in 2026.

## The Witch's Hut and Card Builds

As you gain XP and increase your **Tier** (visible in the 'M' menu), you unlock card slots. You can have a maximum of three slots at Tier 3. To fill these slots, you must visit the Witch in the swamp.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Aging is a real mechanic. Every time you die, your character ages. If you become too old, you may experience stat penalties. Use the **Dog Bane herb** (found via fishing or boss drops) at the Witch's Hut to de-age by 20 years.

### Recommended Card Combinations
The Witch allows you to "read your fortune" for a small Moola fee to roll for passive cards.

| Card Name | Effect | Build Type |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Sturdy** | Increased health and posture. | Defensive / Tank |
| **Quick Draw** | Faster weapon draw and reload speeds. | Gunslinger / PvP |
| **Desperado** | Increased damage when at low health. | High Risk / Reward |
| **High Noon** | Buffs Ocular Prowess accuracy. | Sniper / Aim Assist |

One popular "meta" build involves combining **High Noon** and **Evil Eye**. This combination significantly enhances the Ocular Prowess ability, creating a semi-aimbot effect that makes hitting moving targets much easier for new players.

## Advanced Tips for the Frontier

*   **Horse Tech:** When traveling through water on a horse, hold **Shift + Space**. This allows your horse to "gallop" through water much faster than normal swimming.
*   **The Malcolm 70:** Once you are comfortable with the game, save up for the Malcolm 70. It is a sniper rifle that can one-shot many players if they aren't wearing a Cowboy Hat.
*   **Spawn Points:** Don't forget to set your spawn! You can do this at the Hotel in Ridgeby County or at various NPC camps in the desert. This prevents you from spawning in the middle of nowhere after a defeat.
*   **Join a Gang:** Use the 'M' menu to enter a Gang ID. Playing with a group is the best way to secure Corpse Parts, as your teammates can defend you while the part merges.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I get more Moola quickly in Bridger Western?**

A: The most effective way for beginners is fishing at the Fishing Village. Selling a full inventory of fish can net you 1,000 to 5,000 Moola. For more experienced players, hunting Bandit bosses or winning PvP duels in the tunnel are viable options.

**Q: What is the best Stand for a beginner to use?**

A: Silver Chariot and Star Platinum are excellent for beginners due to their straightforward melee combos and high defensive potential. If you prefer range, Hierophant Green provides great utility.

**Q: Why did my character turn white and start glowing?**

A: This means you are holding a Corpse Part. You must survive until it "merges" with you to receive a Stand. Be careful, as the glow makes you visible to everyone on the map.
