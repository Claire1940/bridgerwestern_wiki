When you arrive at the Green Lands, find an open patch of grass. Upon planting the seed, a growth timer begins. In 2026, the growth process is relatively quick, but it is not without its dangers. The act of planting such a rare specimen often attracts unwanted attention from the local fauna and NPCs.

| Phase | Duration | Expected Outcome |

> **Tip:** If you are looking to speedrun the process, stay close to your plant. Leaving the area might result in the plant despawning or being destroyed by roaming mobs.

## Defending Your Harvest from Mobs

One of the unique mechanics in Bridger Western is the "Guardian" effect. When you are planting a **rokakaka fruit bridger western**, there is a high probability that hostile NPCs, such as zombies or outlaws, will spawn nearby. These enemies are specifically coded to target the player or the plant.

If a zombie spawns on you during the growth phase, you have two choices:

A single successfully grown seed typically yields three full fruits. If you manage your time correctly, a full cycle from chopping wood to harvesting fruit should take approximately 20 to 25 minutes.
