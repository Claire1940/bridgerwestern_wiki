<Section icon={Target} title="Fishing Mechanics and Bait Efficiency">

The **bridger western fishing rod** operates on a simple "wait and react" system, but there are hidden mechanics that determine how fast you can cycle through catches. Understanding the bubble system is key to maximizing your Mula per hour.

When you cast your line, you must wait for white particles or "bubbles" to appear on the water's surface. This indicates a fish is interested. Without bait, these bubbles appear at a standard rate. However, by keeping bait in your inventory, the spawn rate of these particles is doubled. This is the single most important tip for anyone serious about farming Arrow Shards.

| Feature | Standard Fishing | With Bait (Recommended) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Bubble Spawn Speed | 1.0x (Base) | 2.0x (Double Speed) |
| Inventory Requirement | Rod Only | Rod + Bait in Hotbar |
| Primary Cost | 150 Mula (Rod) | Rod + Variable Bait Cost |
| Best Use Case | Casual Play | Farming Rare Loot/Stands |

<Callout type="tip" title="Bait Management">
You do not need to click or use the bait item. As long as it sits in your active hotbar, the 2x speed buff is applied automatically. Check your inventory frequently to ensure you haven't run out during long sessions.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Legendary Loot and Mini-games">

The true power of the **bridger western fishing rod** lies in the mini-game rewards. Occasionally, instead of a fish, you will trigger a special event. These events have three main outcomes: a Chest, a Mar's Leg, or the coveted Arrow Shard.

### The Fishing Chest
Chests contain a variety of supplies that can save you significant Mula in the long run. Common drops include Molotovs, Dynamites, and Ammo packs. However, the "holy grail" of the chest is the **Dog Bane**.

| Item | Source | Effect | Value |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Dog Bane** | Chest | Resets Age to 20 / Resets Stand | 2.5k Mula (Sell to Witch) |
| **Ammo Packs** | Chest | Refills various weapon types | Saves Mula |
| **Explosives** | Chest | Molotovs and Dynamites | Combat Utility |

### Rare Combat Items
If you are looking to upgrade your character's power, fishing provides two unique pathways:

1.  **Rusty Mar's Leg**: This can be turned into a functional firearm for 1.5k Mula. It deals 21 body damage, making it a reliable mid-tier weapon.
2.  **Arrow Shard**: This is the most sought-after item. With a **0.5% drop rate**, it is the safest way to obtain a Stand.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Mastering the Dog Bane and Age Reset">

One of the most unique mechanics in Bridger is the aging system. As your character gets older, you may want to reset your stats or prepare for a new Stand. The Dog Bane, found via the **bridger western fishing rod** chest, is the key to this process.

If your character is 31 years old and you use the Dog Bane, you will revert to age 20 (the minimum age). This is crucial for players who want to maintain a specific character build. Furthermore, if you are trying to obtain a "Corpse Part" but already have a Stand, you must use the Dog Bane to reset your current Stand first. 

If you don't need the reset, the Dog Bane is still incredibly valuable. You can sell it to the Witch NPC for **2,500 Mula**, making it one of the best ways to get rich quickly without engaging in high-risk PvP combat.

| Reset Type | Requirement | Result |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Age Reset** | Dog Bane | Returns character to age 20 |
| **Stand Reset** | Dog Bane | Removes current Stand to allow for Corpse Part acquisition |
| **Mula Profit** | Sell to Witch | 2,500 Mula gain |

<Callout type="info" title="Expert Advice">
Always keep at least one Dog Bane in your storage. You never know when you'll need to reset a Stand to try a new build or fix an accidental age progression.
</Callout>
