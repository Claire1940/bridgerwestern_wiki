## The Requirements for Obtaining Tusk

To unlock Tusk, you cannot rely on luck with standard arrows or random shards. The process is strictly tied to a specific body part from the Saint's Corpse. You must locate a **Left Arm of the Saint**. While other parts like the Rib Cage or Right Arm exist, they will not grant you Tusk; they are used for other Special Stands like D4C or Scary Monsters.

Once you have obtained a Left Arm, using it does not guarantee the Stand. There is a specific success rate you must overcome.

| Item Required | Drop Source | Success Chance | Stand Result |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Left Arm of the Saint** | Corpse Part Spawns | **10%** | Tusk (Act 1) |
| **Saint's Rib Cage** | Corpse Part Spawns | 0% | Unlocks D4C |
| **Saint's Right Arm** | Corpse Part Spawns | 0% | Unlocks Other Stands |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Obtaining Tusk will immediately delete all weapons in your inventory, including guns and sabers. You will also lose the ability to walk or run normally, forcing you to crawl or roll unless you are on horseback.

## Understanding the Tusk Evolution Acts

Tusk is unique because it functions through different "Acts." In the current 2026 version of the game, players can cycle through Acts 1, 2, and 3 by pressing the **V key**. Each Act changes your moveset and damage output significantly. While Act 4 is highly anticipated, the current gameplay focuses on mastering the utility of the first three stages.

### Tusk Act 1: The Mobility Phase
Act 1 is the entry-level form. While its damage is lower than the later stages, it offers the most mobility options for a "crippled" user. It features the **Nail Glide** and a unique small jump that helps navigate terrain.

### Tusk Act 2: The Precision Sniper
Act 2 shifts the focus toward high-damage single shots. This form introduces the **Spatial Wormhole** mechanic, where missed shots can actually home in on enemies or act as traps.

### Tusk Act 3: The High-DPS Finisher
Act 3 provides the highest raw damage per nail shot. It is often considered the most powerful for stationary combat or when you have a teammate protecting you.

| Feature | Act 1 | Act 2 | Act 3 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Primary Fire** | Rapid Fire (Low DMG) | Homing Nails (Mid DMG) | Double-Hit Nails (High DMG) |
| **Special Move** | **Small Jump** | **Sniper Ricochet** | **Enhanced Wormhole** |
| **Movement** | Nail Glide | Nail Glide | Nail Glide |

## Survival Strategies for Tusk Users

Because Tusk renders your character unable to walk, you are extremely vulnerable to players using faster Stands like Star Platinum or The World. To survive as Johnny Joestar’s successor, you must change your approach to the game.

1.  **Invest in a Legendary Horse:** Your horse is your only viable method of fast transportation. Since you cannot run, a high-speed [legendary mount](https://www.roblox.com/games/14532155700/bridger-WESTERN) is mandatory for repositioning during a fight.
2.  **Use the Nail Glide Wisely:** The Nail Glide (V) allows you to move quickly across the ground for a short duration. Use this to create distance when an enemy gets too close.
3.  **Team Up:** Tusk is a "Glass Cannon" Stand. Having a friend with a defensive Stand or a healer can keep you alive while you dish out massive damage from the backline.
4.  **Master the Melee:** Even though you are crippled, you have a melee attack (T key) across all Acts. Use it as a last resort if an enemy manages to close the gap.

## How to Farm Corpse Parts Efficiently

Since the Left Arm has a low spawn rate and an even lower success rate for the Stand, farming in public servers can be frustrating. Many veteran players utilize private servers to "flood" the map with spawns.

*   **Private Server Rotation:** Join a dedicated community or Discord group where players take turns collecting corpse parts.
*   **The "Left Arm Priority" List:** When farming with a group, establish a list. If a Left Arm drops, the person at the top of the Tusk list takes it. If a Rib Cage drops, it goes to the D4C seekers.
*   **Patience is Key:** With a 10% success rate, it is statistically likely that you may need 10 or more Left Arms before Tusk finally awakens.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I use guns while I have Tusk?**

A: No. Upon obtaining Tusk, the game will automatically remove all firearms and melee weapons from your inventory. You are restricted to using Nail Bullets and your Stand's abilities.

**Q: How do I fix the "Crippled" movement if I don't like Tusk?**

A: To regain the ability to walk and use weapons, you must use a **Log Main Herb** to remove the Stand entirely. However, this will delete Tusk, and you will have to farm the Left Arm again if you want it back.

**Q: Is Tusk Act 4 in the game yet?**

A: As of the current 2026 update, Tusk Act 4 is not yet implemented. Players are limited to switching between Acts 1, 2, and 3.

**Q: What is the fastest way for how to get tusk in bridger western?**

A: The fastest method is joining a private server with friends and specifically hunting for the Saint's Left Arm. Ensure you have no other Stand active before attempting to use the arm for the best results.
