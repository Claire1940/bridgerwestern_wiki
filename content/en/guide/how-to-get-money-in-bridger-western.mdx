## The Fishing Meta: Maximum Efficiency at the Digsite

Many veteran players agree that fishing is currently the most consistent way to build wealth. However, simply standing by any body of water is not enough. To truly master **how to get money in bridger western**, you must position yourself at the river near the Digsite.

This specific location is considered the "gold standard" for several reasons. First, the Digsite is close to an ammo vendor, allowing you to quickly restock without losing your spot. Second, the river is a hotspot for NPC invasions. While your line is in the water, bandits and other hostiles will periodically spawn. Killing these NPCs provides a secondary income stream of Moola and XP while you wait for a bite.

> **💡 Tip:** Always keep an eye on your inventory for rare herbs caught while fishing. These can be sold to the Witch NPC for a staggering 2.5k Moola each, significantly boosting your hourly profit.

## Advanced Combat Farming: Bandits and Zombies

If you prefer a more active playstyle, farming encounters in the Desert Badlands is a highly effective alternative. This method relies on the "stacking" mechanic, where you engage in combat while simultaneously performing passive tasks like fishing.

In the Badlands, you will encounter desert bandits during the day and zombies during the night. Each encounter can yield between 100 and 195 XP, along with a variable amount of Moola. By fishing in the Badlands' water sources, you create a cycle where you are never idle. This method can yield approximately 1,000 XP and a significant amount of cash every eight minutes if executed correctly.

## The Outlaw Hideout Cage Method

For players with a dedicated partner or an alternate account, the Outlaw Hideout offers the fastest possible progression for both money and experience. This method involves using the cage system found within the hideout.

1.  **Preparation**: Ensure your partner or alt account is an Outlaw.
2.  **Spawn Setting**: Have the Outlaw account set their spawn at the hideout for 150 Moola.
3.  **The Transfer**: Give the majority of your Moola to the alt account, leaving yourself with a base amount (around 300).
4.  **The Cycle**: Enter the cages together. When the bell rings, have the alt account "reset" or leave.
5.  **The Reward**: You will receive 50 XP and 300 Moola per cycle.

This method is incredibly fast, allowing for up to five cycles per minute. In a focused session, you can amass tens of thousands of XP and Moola in just a few hours. However, be careful not to punch the alt account, as getting them "in combat" may force them to lose their spawn point, breaking the cycle.
