## Getting Started: Character Creation and Controls

When you first launch the game, your journey begins with character customization and alignment selection. You can choose between being an **In-law** or an **Outlaw**. As of the 2026 updates, alignment serves more as a social role-play element than a rigid mechanical constraint, though it may influence how certain NPCs react to you in future expansions.

Once you spawn, be prepared for immediate action. The world is open and often violent; you might find yourself in the middle of a gunfight the moment you appear. Mastering the movement and combat controls is your first line of defense.

| Action | Control Key | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **Sprint** | Double-tap **W** | Increases movement speed and automatically vaults over small obstacles. |
| **Dodge Roll** | **Q** | Provides a brief window of invulnerability and repositioning. |
| **Shoot/Attack** | **M1** (Left Click) | Fires your equipped firearm or swings a melee weapon. |
| **Aim** | **M2** (Right Click) | Tightens your crosshair for better precision at range. |
| **Reload** | **R** | Replenishes your ammunition from your inventory packs. |
| **Melee Bash** | **G** | Uses the butt of your gun to strike nearby enemies. |
| **Ocular Pro** | **X** | Activates a special vision mode that highlights players and boosts accuracy. |
| **Summon Horse** | **H** | Calls your trusty steed to your current location. |
| **Dismount** | **N** | Safely exits your horse. |

> **💡 Tip:** Use the **F** key while sprinting on a horse to perform a "Full Stop." This is vital for stopping exactly where you want without sliding past your destination.

## The Fastest Way to Level Up: The Fishing Meta

While PvP and random world events offer excitement, the most reliable way to progress in **bridget western** is through fishing. This "chill" method allows you to accumulate massive amounts of "Moola" (the in-game currency) and experience points without the constant threat of high-level bounty hunters.

To start your fishing career, follow the train tracks or hop on the train at Ridge B County until you reach the **Fishing Village**. Look for the **Taco Bait Shop** NPC. Here, you should purchase a fishing rod and plenty of bait.

### Fishing Rewards and Economy

Fishing isn't just about the fish; it's about the rare loot that comes with it. You can find items ranging from common Cod to rare chests containing dynamite and steel wires.

| Item Type | Estimated Value | Special Properties |
|---|---|---|
| **Common Fish** | 50 - 200 Moola | Good for consistent early-game income. |
| **Rare Fish (e.g., U-Bats)** | 500 - 900 Moola | High-value catches that boost your bank account quickly. |
| **Dogs Bane Herb** | N/A | **Crucial:** Use this to reduce your character's age after dying. |
| **Stand Arrow** | Priceless | 0.5% drop rate; used to unlock your first Stand ability. |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Aging is a real mechanic in this game. Every time you die, your character ages. If you get too old, you may suffer stat penalties, so always keep Dogs Bane in your inventory.

## Mastery of the Frontier: Horses and Weaponry

Traveling across the vast biomes of **bridget western** on foot is a recipe for disaster. You need a fast horse and a reliable gun. In Ridge B County, you will find the Horse Dealer NPC near the train station.

### Choosing the Right Horse

Don't settle for the cheapest mule. High-quality horses have significantly better stamina and top speeds. 

*   **The 1k Rule:** Always aim to buy a horse that costs at least **1,100 Moola**.
*   **Re-rolling:** If the dealer offers a low-stat horse, decline and re-roll until you see a price tag above 1k.
*   **Water Tech:** While riding, hold **Shift + Space** when entering water. This allows your horse to "gallop" through the water at high speeds rather than slowly swimming.

### Essential Weaponry

You can purchase firearms at the local Gun Shop or the Outlaw Hideout. Each weapon serves a specific purpose in the 2026 meta.

1.  **Pistols/Colts:** Reliable sidearms for quick encounters.
2.  **Winchester/Maverick:** Excellent for mid-to-long-range engagements.
3.  **Double Barrel Shotgun:** The king of close-quarters combat inside buildings.
4.  **Tommy Gun:** High fire rate, perfect for suppressing groups of enemies.

## Unlocking Inner Power: Stands and Cards

The "JoJo" in this JoJo-themed western comes from **Stands** and **Passive Cards**. These are the elements that truly separate the pros from the amateurs.

### How to Get a Stand

There are two primary ways to obtain a Stand in **bridget western**:
*   **Fishing:** As mentioned, there is a 0.5% chance to pull a Stand Arrow from the water.
*   **Corpse Parts:** This is a faster but much more dangerous method. Corpse parts spawn every 30 seconds in random locations. The more players in a server, the faster they spawn. However, these locations are often "hot zones" where multiple players will fight to the death for a single part.

### The Witch’s Fortune: Passive Cards

Deep in the swamp biome, you will find the **Witch’s Hut**. This NPC is the only way to unlock your passive cards. You can have a maximum of three cards, which are unlocked as you "Tier Up" (level up).

| Card Name | Effect |
|---|---|
| **High Noon** | Enhances Ocular Pro capabilities. |
| **Evil Eye** | Increases critical hit chance during combat. |
| **Secret Technique** | Provides unique movement or defensive buffs. |

> **💡 Tip:** Certain card combinations can create an "Aimbot" build. For example, combining **High Noon** with **Evil Eye** while using **Ocular Pro (X)** allows your bullets to slightly curve toward targets, making it almost impossible to miss.

## Exploring the Map: Key Landmarks

Navigating the world is easier if you use the train tracks as your primary guide. Most major locations are situated near the tracks or within a short ride from a station.

1.  **Ridge B County:** The central hub for banking, basic gun shops, and clothing.
2.  **Fishing Village/Taco Bait Shop:** The primary area for economy and early leveling.
3.  **Outlaw Hideout:** Located straight past the bait shop; sells specialized items like steel wires and explosives.
4.  **The Swamp:** Home to the Witch’s Hut for card management.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I reduce my character's age in bridget western?**

A: You must find or catch a **Dogs Bane** herb. Consuming this item will lower your age, preventing the negative effects associated with getting too old after multiple deaths.

**Q: What is the maximum level or tier I can reach?**

A: Currently, the max tier is **Tier 3**. Reaching this level allows you to equip a total of three passive cards from the Witch's Hut.

**Q: Can I change my Stand after I’ve unlocked one?**

A: Yes, but you will need a new Stand Arrow or a specific reset item. Most players choose to farm arrows through fishing or compete for Corpse Parts to change their abilities.

**Q: Where can I buy more ammo?**

A: Ammo packs can be purchased at any general store, gun shop, or found at bandit outposts scattered throughout the map. Simply left-click the ammo pack in your inventory to refill your reserves.
