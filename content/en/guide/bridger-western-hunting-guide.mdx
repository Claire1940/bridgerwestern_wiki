## Getting Started: The Resource Hunt

Before you can hunt for the legendary Saint’s Corpse, you must first hunt for basic resources. Upon spawning, your alignment (Outlaw or In-law) serves mostly as a roleplay element in the current 2026 build, so focus instead on reaching Ridgeby County. The easiest way to navigate is to follow the train tracks; they act as the main artery of the map.

Your first objective is the Fishing Village. Fishing is the most reliable, "chill" way to level up and earn cash without the constant threat of high-stakes PvP. Visit the Taco Bait Shop and purchase a fishing rod and bait from the NPC.

### Fishing Economy and Leveling

| Item | Purpose | Acquisition |
|---|---|---|
| **Fishing Rod** | Essential tool for catching fish and items | Purchased at Taco Bait Shop |
| **Bait** | Increases catch rate and quality | Purchased at Taco Bait Shop |
| **Dog's Bane** | Herb used to reduce character age | Rare catch while fishing |
| **Stand Arrow** | Unlocks a random Stand (0.5% chance) | Very rare catch while fishing |

Selling your catch can net you thousands of moola quickly. For example, a stack of Cod or U-Bats can easily provide 5,000 moola, which is more than enough to fund your initial gear and horses.

> **💡 Tip:** Every time you die, your character's age increases. Use Dog's Bane herbs found through fishing to lower your age and avoid the penalties associated with getting too old.

## Mobility: Hunting for the Perfect Steed

In Bridger Western, your horse is your lifeline. To summon your horse, press **H**. While every beginner starts with a basic mule, you should upgrade as soon as you have 1,000 moola. Visit the Stable NPC in Ridgeby County, located directly in front of the train station.

When purchasing a horse, do not settle for the cheap options. Reroll the NPC's offerings until you see a horse priced at **1,100 moola or higher**. These horses possess significantly better speed and stamina stats.

### Horse Mastery Techniques
*   **Sprinting:** Hold **Shift** to gallop.
*   **Quick Stop:** Press **F** to stop instantly while sprinting.
*   **Water Travel:** While on your horse in the water, hold **Space** to move significantly faster across rivers and swamps.
*   **Dismount:** Press **N** to hop off.

## The Ultimate Hunt: Saint's Corpse Parts

The most prestigious activity in any **bridger western hunting guide** is the Saint's Corpse hunt. These parts are the primary way to obtain powerful Stands. Unlike fishing for arrows, corpse parts spawn based on server activity and player count.

### Spawn Mechanics and Probability
A global message and a giant beam of light signal a corpse part spawn. The frequency depends on the number of players in the server:

| Player Count | Spawn Chance (Every 30 Seconds) |
|---|---|
| **3+ Players** | 1 in 150 |
| **Full Server** | **1 in 40** |

### Key Spawn Locations
1.  **The Dig Site:** Located at the bottom of a large excavation area. It is difficult to escape, making it a dangerous "kill box."
2.  **Death Valley Rock:** A high-vantage point in the desert. You can reach it by standing on your horse and using the recoil of a shotgun or revolver to "boost" upward.
3.  **The Watchtower:** Located near the fishing area. This is a highly defensible position but leaves you vulnerable while climbing the ladder.
4.  **Ant Hill Island:** A small island near the fishing village. It is often overlooked by casual players.
5.  **The Swamp:** Spawns in the center of the murky waters near the Witch’s Hut.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Once you pick up a corpse part, you must "merge" with it. This takes between 10 seconds and 2 minutes. During this time, you are a high-priority target for every other player in the server.

## Combat and the "Aimbot" Meta

Hunting players or defending your corpse part requires mastery of the Ocular Proess system. By pressing **X**, you enter a heightened state that increases accuracy, highlights enemies, and boosts reload speed. 

To truly excel, you must visit the Witch in the swamp to unlock Passive Cards. You can have up to three cards active once you reach Tier 3.

### The Witch's Card System
Each roll at the Witch costs 50 moola (the first is free). You can reject fates you don't like to keep searching for the "God Tier" combinations.

| Card Combination | Effect |
|---|---|
| **High Noon + Evil Eye + Folks High Noon** | Grants literal **Aimbot** capabilities during Ocular Proess. |
| **Tonic Secret Technique** | Increases healing efficiency from tonics. |
| **Total Checkmate** | Enhances damage output during critical encounters. |

## Arsenal: Hunting for Better Firepower

While the starter weapons are functional, you will eventually need to upgrade your kit. There are two primary locations to purchase firearms:

1.  **Ridgeby County Gun Shop:** Sells the Winchester, Maverick, and Tommy Gun.
2.  **Outlaw Hideout:** Located straight past the Taco Bait Shop. The NPC here sells the Colt and the Double Barrel Shotgun. He also sells utility items like **Steel Wires**, Dynamite, and Molotovs.

To keep your weapons firing, always carry **Ammo Packs**, which can be purchased at any general store or looted from bandit outposts scattered across the map. ## FAQ

**Q: How do I get the Tusk Stand in Bridger Western?**

A: Tusk is unique because it can only be obtained from the **Left Arm** corpse part, which has a 10% chance to grant Tusk specifically. Other corpse parts provide a random pool of Stands.

**Q: What is the fastest way to level up my Tier?**

A: While this **bridger western hunting guide** recommends fishing for a safe experience, participating in random world events and winning PvP duels provides the fastest Tier experience, though at a much higher risk of death and aging.

**Q: Can I get Stands without fighting for Corpse Parts?**

A: Yes, you can catch Stand Arrows while fishing (0.5% chance). However, the rarest Stands like Star Platinum or The World AU are much more likely to come from Saint's Corpse parts.

**Q: Where can I find the Heart corpse part?**

A: The Heart is a secret corpse part used specifically for obtaining the D4C Stand. Its acquisition is significantly more complex than the standard five parts and usually requires a specific questline or extremely rare world event participation.
