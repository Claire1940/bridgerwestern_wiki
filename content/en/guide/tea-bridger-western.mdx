## Harvesting Strategies and Set Spawns

Finding these plants is the most time-consuming aspect of the **tea bridger western** loop. Unlike other resources that might have randomized locations, these herbs often appear in set spawns within the desert biome. 

### Desert Navigation

The desert is the primary source of all herbal life in the current build of the game. When searching, focus your attention on the base of cacti. Many of the most valuable sprouts, such as the Hellfire Sprout, grow in the shade provided by these large desert plants. Furthermore, look for patches of disturbed ground or small rock outcroppings, as roots like the Reservoir Root tend to grow deep beneath the surface in these areas.

### Gathering Routes

1.  **The Cactus Run:** Start at the southern edge of the desert and move north, checking every large Saguaro cactus. This is the most efficient way to find Hawkeye and Hellfire variants.
2.  **The Dry Riverbed:** Follow the path of the dried-up rivers. The soil here often retains enough moisture to support Turtle Shell Stems and Reservoir Roots.
3.  **The High Ridges:** Deer Heart Root is more commonly found at slightly higher elevations where the desert meets the foothills.

> **⚠️ Warning:** The desert is a high-PvP zone. Gathering herbs makes you a target for bandits who would rather steal your hard-earned plants than find their own.
