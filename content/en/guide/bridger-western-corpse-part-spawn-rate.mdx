## Primary Corpse Part Spawn Locations

There are approximately eight major spawn points currently recognized by the community in 2026. Knowing these by heart is the first step to improving your effective **bridger western corpse part spawn rate** per hour.

### 1. Taco Bay Tower and Surrounds
Taco Bay is the central hub for most item hunters. There are three distinct spawn points in this vicinity:
*   **The Tower:** A Saint’s Part frequently appears at the very top of the wooden tower. This is highly visible, making it a flashpoint for combat.
*   **Across the Shop:** Directly across from the Taco Bay bait shop, a part can spawn on the ground near the wooden structures.
*   **The Bridge/Tracks:** Following the train tracks near the fishing village often reveals spawns tucked near the supports.

### 2. The Dig Site (Rich B County)
Located in Rich B County, the Dig Site is notorious for being a "death trap." While the spawn rate here is decent, the geography works against you. 
> **Warning:** The Dig Site is a deep pit. To exit, you must climb a slow-moving rope. If a Corpse Part spawns here, expect a "full-on PVP" scenario where players will jump down to swarm the item, leaving you with no easy escape route.

### 3. The Wilderness and Desert
Between the major landmarks lies the vast wilderness. Spawns here are often found behind large rock formations or near the base of cliffs. These are harder to pinpoint but are less likely to be camped by large groups.

### 4. The Valley
The Valley is the furthest point from the main spawn. Because of its distance, many players ignore it, which technically increases your personal success rate if you are willing to make the trip. The fastest way to reach this area is by utilizing the train system, as walking across the desert is inefficient.

## Strategies to Increase Your Success Rate

Finding a Corpse Part is often more about strategy than luck. Relying solely on the base **bridger western corpse part spawn rate** in a single server is the slowest way to progress. Instead, veteran players use the following methods:

This involves checking the high-value spawns (like the Taco Bay Tower) and immediately leaving the server to join a different one if no part is present. This allows you to check dozens of "spawn windows" in the time it would take to wait for one spawn in a single server.

### Discord Rotations
The most effective way to "guarantee" a part is through community coordination. Many top-tier players join dedicated Discord servers (such as the Jozu server) to participate in "Bridge Floods" or organized rotations.
*   **Rotations:** A group of players monitors multiple servers and alerts the group when a part spawns.
*   **Private Servers:** Farming in a private server eliminates the PVP risk, though it requires a Robux investment or a friend with access.
