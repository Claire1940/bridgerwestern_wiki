## Survival Tips for the Western Frontier

Beyond the supernatural elements, **bridger western** is still a survival game at its core. You must manage your health and resources while fending off other players who may want to steal your loot.

*   **Group Up:** Farming is always safer in a posse. Having friends to watch your back while you wait for item spawns prevents "spawn killing" by opportunistic players.
