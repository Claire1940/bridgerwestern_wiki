## Early Game Progression: Funding Your Gang

No gang can survive without "Moola." In Bridger Western, the most reliable way to build your initial fortune is through the fishing mechanic. Located at the Fishing Village (often reached by following the train tracks to the forest biome), fishing allows you to level up your Tier and earn cash safely.

### How to Start Fishing
1.  **Locate the Taco Bait Shop:** Follow the train tracks until they stop at the station near the forest biome.
2.  **Purchase Gear:** Buy a fishing rod and several units of bait from the NPC.
3.  **Sell Your Catch:** Higher-tier fish like Cod can fetch upwards of **1.5k Moola**, which is vital for buying your first high-quality horse.

> **💡 Tip:** Fishing has a 0.5% chance to drop a Stand Arrow Shard. While rare, it is a safe alternative to fighting over Corpse Parts in high-traffic zones.

## The Importance of High-Tier Horses

In the **bridger western gang system**, mobility is your greatest asset. A slow horse makes you an easy target for snipers and rival gang members. When visiting the horse NPC in Richby County, do not settle for the cheapest option.

| Horse Quality | Price Range | Stats/Speed |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Starter (Mule)** | Free / Low Cost | **Very Slow, Low Stamina** |
| **Mid-Tier** | 500 - 900 Moola | **Average Speed, Reliable** |
| **Legendary/High-Tier** | 1,000 - 1,500 Moola | **Max Speed, High Health** |

Always aim for a horse in the **1k+ Moola** range. You can "reroll" the NPC's offering for a small fee until a high-stat horse appears. Additionally, master the "Horse Tech" by sprinting into water and spamming the **Space Bar** to traverse rivers quickly—a vital skill for escaping ambushes.

## Unlocking Cards and Talents at the Witch's Hut

The true power of any player within the **bridger western gang system** comes from their Card build. Cards provide passive buffs that can drastically alter combat outcomes. To unlock these, you must visit the Witch's Hut located deep within the swamp area.

### Card Slot Mechanics
Your Tier (level) determines how many cards you can equip simultaneously. The maximum Tier is 3, allowing for a total of three active cards.

*   **First Card:** Free to unlock.
*   **Second/Third Cards:** Cost **50 Moola** each to reveal.
*   **Rerolling:** If you dislike a card, you can "Reject this Fate" and pay for a new one.

### Popular Combat Synergies
By combining specific cards, you can unlock hidden abilities. For example, pairing **High Noon** with **Evil Eye** and **Folks' High Noon** allows you to utilize an "Aimbot" style mechanic while using the Ocular Prowess (X key) ability. This makes you a formidable force in any gang war.

## Stands and Corpse Parts: The Gang’s Heavy Artillery

The most sought-after items in Bridger Western are Corpse Parts. When the message "It appears once again" flashes in gold at the top of the screen, a Corpse Part has spawned. These are marked by a massive golden beam of light.

### Obtaining a Stand
1.  **The Pulse:** Once you pick up a Corpse Part, you must hold it. Every "pulse" gives a **15% to 20% chance** to obtain a Stand.
2.  **Server Scaling:** In a server with 25 players, a Corpse Part spawns every 30 seconds with a **1 out of 40 chance**. In smaller servers, the odds decrease significantly.
3.  **The Risk:** Carrying a Corpse Part highlights you to the entire server. Expect every rival gang to descend on your location immediately.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Combat logging (quitting during a fight) will increase your character's age by **10 years**. Aging causes facial wrinkles and gray hair, and while the community debates if "natural death" exists, it is best to avoid the penalty.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I join the Bridger Western gang system as an Outlaw?**

A: When creating your character, select the "Outlaw" alignment. You can then congregate with other Outlaws at the hideout in the swamp area to coordinate attacks or trade specialized gear like steel wires and dynamite.

**Q: What happens when my character gets old?**

A: Every time you die, your age increases by 1-2 years. Combat logging increases it by 10. As you age, your character will develop wrinkles and gray hair. You can reduce your age by using the **Dog's Bane** herb, which can be found while fishing or exploring the map.

**Q: Can I change my Stand once I have one?**

A: Yes, but you must reset your current Stand first. This can be done by "wiping" your character or by finding a specific plant through fishing and bringing it to the Witch in the swamp.

**Q: What is the fastest way to level up my Tier?**

A: Fishing is the safest and most consistent way to earn XP (Tier progress) for beginners. However, participating in World Events and winning PvP encounters against rival gang members provides faster progression for experienced players.
