## The Trading System: A Better Way to Drop

Frequently, the reason players search for **how to drop stuff in bridger western console** is to give items to other players. To initiate a trade on console, you must approach another player and interact with their "Trade Stand." This creates a secure window where items like Rokakku seeds or Stand Arrows can be exchanged without the risk of them disappearing into the terrain.

### How to Trade on Console
1.  **Approach the Player:** Walk up to the player you wish to trade with.
2.  **Open the Trade Menu:** Press the interaction button (X on Xbox / Square on PS) when the prompt appears over their character.
3.  **Select Your Item:** Use the thumbstick to highlight the item in your inventory you wish to give away.
4.  **Confirm the Offer:** Both players must accept the trade. A 5-second countdown usually occurs to prevent "ninja-trading" or last-second swaps.
5.  **Finalize:** Once the timer expires, the items are moved directly into the other player's inventory.

**Q: How do I drop items specifically on Xbox?**

A: To drop a held item on Xbox, equip it to your hand and hold the **B button**. If you are in the inventory menu, highlight the item and look for the "Drop" prompt at the bottom of the screen, usually mapped to the **X button**.

A: Stands (like White Snake or The World) are not physical items in your inventory and cannot be "dropped." To give a Stand to another player, you must use the specific Stand Trading interface or use a Rokakku to reset your Stand and then trade the resulting item if applicable.
