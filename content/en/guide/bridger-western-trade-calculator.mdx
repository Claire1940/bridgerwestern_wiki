<Section icon={Map} title="Acquiring the Rokakaka Fruit: Your Trading Key">
The Rokakaka Fruit is the lynchpin of the trading system in Bridger Western. Without it, initiating a trade is impossible. Understanding its acquisition process is the first step to becoming a proficient trader. This valuable fruit is obtained through the game's tree chopping system, primarily found in specific regions.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Outlaw Regions Advantage">

  <Card title="Lawkeeper Access">
    - **Travel westward from Ridge County** to find these regions.
    - Requires a bit more travel for Inlaws (Lawkeepers).
    - The effort is worthwhile for trading potential.

To begin your Rokakaka Fruit hunt, you'll need a Lumber Axe. Head to the river and locate the Lumber NPC, who resides in a hut across the bridge. This NPC sells the Lumber Axe for 250 Moola. Once equipped, start chopping trees. The drop rate for a Rokakaka Seed can be low, so persistence is key. After acquiring a seed, plant it in the Rokakaka ground at Grasslands, and in just 30 seconds, it will mature into a usable Rokakaka Fruit.

<Callout type="warning" title="Patience is Key">
The Rokakaka Seed has a low drop chance. Don't get discouraged if it takes several attempts to acquire one. Consistent tree chopping will eventually yield results.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Executing a Trade: The Rokakaka Exchange Process">
Once you have a Rokakaka Fruit, the actual trading process is straightforward but requires both parties to meet certain criteria. This ensures fair play and prevents new players from being exploited in high-value exchanges.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Find a Trading Partner">
    Ensure both you and your trading partner are on the same game server. Communication is key to coordinate the trade.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Equip and Offer the Fruit">
    With the Rokakaka Fruit in your inventory, equip it. Then, click on your interested trading partner to split the Fruit, offering one half to them.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Partner Confirmation">
    Your trading partner must confirm the acceptance of their half of the Rokakaka Fruit. This initiates the exchange.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Consumption and Effects">
    Both players consume their respective Rokakaka Fruit halves. This triggers the trade, applying its effects instantly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The Rokakaka Fruit facilitates several types of exchanges. You can swap one or two of your Cards, exchange Stands, or even equalize your in-game ages with your partner. The trade is complete once these effects take place.

**Important Trading Prerequisites:**

| Requirement | Description | Impact on Trade |
| :---------- | :---------- | :------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Tier 3 Status** | Both players must have reached Tier 3. | Ensures a certain level of game progression and understanding. |
| **Own a Stand** | Both players must possess at least one Stand. | Stands are a primary tradable asset, fundamental for the system. |

<Callout type="success" title="Successful Trade">
After a successful trade, you'll immediately see your new Cards or Stand in your inventory, and your age will be adjusted if that was part of the exchange.
