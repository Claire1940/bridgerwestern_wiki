## Getting Started: Character Creation and Controls

Your journey begins with character customization and alignment selection. Currently, in 2026, you can choose to be an **Outlaw** or an **In-law**. While the alignment mechanics are still evolving, your choice sets the tone for your roleplay experience. Once you spawn into the world, you are immediately vulnerable to the chaotic nature of the map. Unlike many other RPGs, Bridger Western has no "newbie protection" zones; you can spawn directly into a heated gunfight.

Before engaging in combat, you must familiarize yourself with the movement and interaction system. The game utilizes a fluid vaulting mechanic that allows you to navigate obstacles seamlessly while sprinting.

| Action | Keybind | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Sprint** | Double-Tap W | Increases movement speed and enables auto-vaulting. |
| **Combat Roll** | Q | A quick dodge to avoid incoming fire or Stand abilities. |

| **Aim** | M2 (Right Click) | Tightens your spread for better accuracy. |
| **Reload** | R | Essential for keeping your firearms ready. |
| **Melee** | G | A quick strike with your weapon's stock or barrel. |
| **Ocular PRO** | X | Highlights enemies and increases reload speed/accuracy. |
| **Summon Horse** | H | Calls your mount to your current location. |

> **Warning:** Aging is a real mechanic in this game. Every time you die, your character's age increases. Keep an eye out for **Dog's Bane** herbs to reduce your age and maintain your peak physical condition.

## The Path to Wealth: Fishing and Leveling

When **a true man approaches Bridger Western**, he realizes that charging into PvP with a basic starter pistol is a recipe for disaster. The most efficient way to gain experience and "Moola" (the in-game currency) is through fishing. This "chill" progression path allows you to bypass the early-game struggle while building up a significant bankroll.

To start, follow the train tracks to **Ridge County** or hop on the train until you reach the forest biome. Locate the **Taco Bait Shop** near the water.

### Fishing Equipment and Economy

| Item | Price | Purpose |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Fishing Rod** | 150 Moola | Basic tool required to catch fish and items. |
| **Bait** | 10 Moola | Increases the frequency of bites. |
| **Tonic** | 50 Moola | Essential healing item for survival. |
| **Ammo Pack** | 75 Moola | Refills your ammunition reserves. |

By selling your catch to the NPC at the bait shop, you can easily earn thousands of Moola in a single session. Rare catches like **Cod** can net you upwards of 1.5k Moola, while junk items pulled from the water might include crafting materials like the **Rusty Mayor's Leg** or even the elusive **Stand Arrow**.

## Mastering the Frontier: When A True Man Approaches Bridger Western

Combat in this game is fast-paced and lethal. To survive, you need more than just quick reflexes; you need the right gear and a solid understanding of the **Ocular PRO** system. Pressing X activates Ocular PRO, which not only highlights players but also grants a significant buff to your reload speed.

### Choosing the Right Horse

Your horse is your lifeline. While every player starts with a basic mule, you should upgrade as soon as possible. Visit the horse merchant in Ridge County, located directly in front of the train station.

> **💡 Tip:** Do not settle for a cheap horse. Reroll the merchant's offerings until you find a horse costing **1,000 Moola or more**. These higher-priced mounts have significantly better stamina and top speeds.

You can also use the "Horse Tech" maneuver: while crossing water, hold **Shift** to sprint and tap **Space** to jump. This allows your horse to glide across water surfaces much faster than swimming normally.

### Weaponry and Shops

There are two primary locations to purchase firearms. The main gun shop in Ridge County sells standard rifles and pistols, while the **Outlaw Hideout** (located through the swamp from the fishing village) offers specialized weaponry.

*   **Winchester:** A reliable all-around rifle for mid-range engagements.
*   **Maverick:** High damage but requires precision.
*   **Double Barrel:** Devastating at close range, perfect for defending against Stand users.
*   **Steel Wires:** A utility tool available at the Outlaw Hideout for 350 Moola.

## Unlocking Supernatural Power: Stands and Cards

The "JoJo" in this JoJo game comes from the inclusion of **Stands**. These powerful spirits provide unique abilities that can turn the tide of any fight. There are two main ways to obtain a Stand:

1.  **Fishing:** There is a 0.5% chance to pull a Stand Arrow from the water.
2.  **Corpse Parts:** These spawn every 30 seconds across the map. The more players in a server, the faster they appear. However, be prepared for a fight, as these items are highly contested.

### The Witch and the Card System

As you "Tier Up" (level up), you unlock slots for **Passive Cards**. You can have a maximum of three cards at Tier 3. To unlock and roll for these cards, you must visit the **Witch's Hut** located deep within the swamp area.

| Card Name | Effect | Synergy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **High Noon** | Increases accuracy during Ocular PRO. | High Synergy with Evil Eye. |
| **Evil Eye** | Increases the duration of Ocular PRO. | Essential for "Aimbot" builds. |
| **Secret Technique** | Enhances movement speed after rolling. | Good for Outlaw playstyles. |
| **Lay of the Tonic** | Increases healing efficiency. | Survival-focused builds. |

By combining **High Noon**, **Evil Eye**, and **Folks High Noon**, players can achieve what is colloquially known as a "Legal Aimbot" build. This combination allows you to land shots even if your reticle isn't perfectly centered on the target while Ocular PRO is active.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I stop my character from aging in Bridger Western?**

A: You cannot completely stop aging, as it occurs every time you die. However, you can find and use **Dog's Bane** herbs to decrease your age and reset your character's physical appearance and stats.

**Q: What is the fastest way to get a Stand?**

A: While fishing is safer, hunting for **Corpse Parts** is significantly faster if you can survive the PvP encounters. Corpse parts spawn frequently, especially in populated servers, but they attract a lot of attention from other players.

**Q: Where is the Outlaw Hideout?**

A: From the fishing village (Taco Bait Shop), head straight into the swamp. Look for the hidden path that leads away from the Witch's Hut. The hideout vendor sells unique items like Steel Wires and specialized shotguns.

**Q: Does the "Aimbot" card build still work when a true man approaches Bridger Western in 2026?**

A: Yes, the combination of High Noon and Evil Eye remains a top-tier meta strategy for players who prefer gunplay over Stand-heavy combat. It requires Tier 3 to fully maximize the synergy.
