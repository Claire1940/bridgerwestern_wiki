## Bridger Western How to Use Bait for Maximum Efficiency

The core question for many new players is often **Bridger Western how to use bait**. The good news is, it's incredibly simple and doesn't require any complex actions or hotkeys beyond having it in your possession.

Here's the straightforward method for using bait:

1.  **Purchase Bait**: Buy bait from the tackle bait shop vendor.
2.  **Keep it in Inventory/Hotbar**: Once purchased, simply ensure the bait is present in your character's inventory or hotbar. You do not need to "equip" it or actively use it like an ability.
3.  **Equip Fishing Rod**: Select and equip your fishing rod.
4.  **Cast Your Line**: Find a body of water (anywhere except the swamp) and cast your line.

The magic of bait happens passively. With bait in your inventory, the "particles" or "bubbles" that indicate a potential bite will appear **twice as fast**. This effectively halves the waiting time between potential catches, dramatically increasing your fishing output and the rate at which you can acquire items.

> **💡 Tip:** Always ensure you have a stock of bait before embarking on a fishing session. The increased speed it provides translates directly into more catches per hour, making your time spent fishing far more productive.

**Q: Where can I buy bait and a fishing rod in Bridger Western?**

A: You can purchase both bait and a fishing rod from the NPC vendor located at the tackle bait shop, which is easily accessible from the docks near Red Corner. A fishing rod costs 150 Moola.
