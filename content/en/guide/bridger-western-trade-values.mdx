## FAQ

**Q: Why is White Snake considered the most valuable stand in Bridger Western?**

A: White Snake combines extreme rarity with high-tier combat capabilities. In the current 2026 meta, it is the hardest Stand to obtain, making its **bridger western trade values** essentially "priceless" as it can be traded for almost any other combination of items in the game.

**Q: Should I trade my Arrows for mid-tier stands?**

A: Generally, no. Most veteran players value Arrows higher than Tier 5 or Tier 4 stands. Because an Arrow has the potential to roll a Tier 1 or Tier 2 Stand, the "opportunity cost" of trading it for a mediocre Stand is usually too high.

**Q: What is the use of the Golden Base in trades?**

A: The Golden Base is a collector's item. While it is rare, it currently has no functional utility in combat or gameplay. Its value is purely cosmetic and subjective, so it is best used as a "filler" in high-end trades rather than a primary trade piece.

**Q: How do I get rid of a Tier 6 Stand that I don't want?**

A: To remove a Tier 6 Stand, you must use a Dogvein Herb. Because these stands are considered useless and occupy a slot that could be used for something better, the cost of the herb actually makes the Stand have "negative" trade value.
