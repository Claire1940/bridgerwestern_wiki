## The Fishing Method: The Path of Patience

If the chaos of PvP isn't your style, Bridger Western offers a more peaceful, albeit much slower, alternative: Fishing. This method allows you to obtain a **Stand Arrow** (often referred to as an Auto Shard), which grants a Stand upon use. While this is the "easiest" method in terms of combat difficulty, the rarity of the drop makes it a true test of endurance.

### How to Start Fishing

Before you can cast a line, you need to visit the **Taco Bait Shop**. For new players, the map can be disorienting. The most reliable way to find the shop is to locate the train tracks and follow them. The train tracks serve as the main artery of the world, connecting major hubs. The Taco Bait Shop is situated near one of the primary train stops.

Once you arrive, you will need to purchase the necessary equipment from the NPC:

| Item | Purpose | Cost/Note |
|---|---|---|
| **Fishing Rod** | The basic tool required to fish. | Essential starter gear. |
| **Bait** | Increases your chances of a bite. | Consumable item. |
| **Auto Shard** | The legendary item that grants a Stand. | **0.5% Drop Rate** from fishing. |

### Fishing Mechanics and Restrictions

Fishing in Bridger Western is a straightforward mini-game. Once you cast your line, wait for the blue indicator to move near your bobber, then left-click to reel it in. While the process is simple, the Stand Arrow/Auto Shard comes with several strict rules that you must follow to avoid losing your progress:

*   **Legendary Rarity:** The drop rate is approximately **0.5%**, meaning you may need to fish for hours before seeing one.
*   **No Banking:** You cannot store the Stand Arrow in a bank or inventory for later use.
*   **Death Penalty:** If you die while holding the Stand Arrow, it is lost forever.
*   **Leave Penalty:** If you leave the server before using the item, it will disappear.
*   **Usage Requirement:** You cannot use a Stand Arrow if you already possess a Stand. You must be "Stand-less" to activate it.

> **💡 Tip:** Always have your horse ready (press **H**) when fishing in open areas. If you finally catch an Auto Shard, you need to use it immediately or get to a safe location to avoid being ambushed by opportunistic players.
