## Getting Started: Character Creation and Controls

When you first load into the game, your first major decision involves choosing an alignment: Outlaw or In-law. While the current 2026 meta doesn't heavily penalize or reward one over the other in terms of raw stats, your alignment sets the tone for your interactions with NPCs and other players. 

After selecting your alignment, you will spawn into a world that can be quite unforgiving. It is not uncommon to spawn directly into a heated gunfight. Mastering the movement system is your first line of defense.

### Basic Movement and Combat Controls

| Action | Keybind | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **Sprint** | Double-tap **W** | Increases movement speed and automatically vaults obstacles. |
| **Dodge Roll** | **Q** | Provides a brief window of invulnerability and repositioning. |
| **Shoot/Use** | **M1** (Left Click) | Fires your equipped weapon or uses an item. |
| **Aim** | **M2** (Right Click) | Tightens your spread and allows for precision shots. |
| **Reload** | **R** | Replenishes your current weapon's magazine. |
| **Melee** | **G** | Performs a physical strike with your weapon equipped. |
| **Ocular Prowess**| **X** | Highlights enemies, increases accuracy, and boosts reload speed. |
| **Summon Horse** | **H** | Calls your mount to your current location. |

> **💡 Tip:** Use the "Horse Tech" by holding **Shift** to sprint and tapping **Space** when entering water. This allows your horse to navigate rivers and lakes much faster than swimming.

## The Economy: Fishing for Fortune

For beginners, the most reliable way to progress in **Western Bridger** is not through combat, but through fishing. Combat can be risky, especially since every death increases your character's age. Fishing provides a safe, consistent stream of income and experience points.

To begin, follow the train tracks until you reach the **Taco Bait Shop** near the fishing village. Purchase a fishing rod and a stack of bait. Once you have your gear, head to the docks and start casting.

## Transportation: Choosing the Right Horse

Your horse is your lifeline. While every player starts with a basic mule, you should look to upgrade as soon as you have saved roughly 1,100 moola. In Ridge County, you can find the Horse NPC near the train station.

Do not settle for the first horse offered if it is cheap. The stats of a horse are often tied to its purchase price. You want to "re-roll" the NPC's offerings until you see a horse priced at **1,000 moola or higher**. These horses possess significantly better stamina and top speeds, which are vital for escaping high-level bounty hunters or catching up to a moving train.

## Combat Mastery: Weapons and Gear

Once you have established a financial cushion, it is time to arm yourself. **Western Bridger** offers a variety of firearms, ranging from standard revolvers to heavy-duty Gatling guns and experimental weaponry.

### Recommended Weaponry for 2026

1.  **Ridge County Gun Shop:** This is the primary hub for standard arms. Here you can purchase the Winchester, Maverick, and the Tommy Gun.
2.  **The Outlaw Hideout:** Located straight past the Taco Bait Shop, this hidden vendor sells specialized gear like the Colt and Double Barrel Shotgun.
3.  **Utility Items:** Always carry a Lasso and a Lantern. The Lasso is essential for non-lethal takedowns, while the Lantern is required for navigating the swamp at night.

> **Warning:** Ammo is not infinite. You must purchase Ammo Packs from shops or scavenge them from bandit outposts. Keep your reserves high before heading into the wilderness.

## The Supernatural: Stands and Cards

The "JoJo" elements of the game are what truly set it apart. Stands provide powerful passive and active abilities that can turn the tide of any duel.

### How to Obtain Stands
There are two primary ways to gain a Stand:
*   **Fishing:** As mentioned, there is a very low (0.5%) chance to pull a Stand Arrow from the water.
*   **Corpse Parts:** These are high-value items that spawn randomly across the map every 30 seconds. The spawn rate increases with the number of players in the server. Be warned: picking up a corpse part usually results in the entire server hunting you down.

### The Witch's Hut and Ability Cards
To further customize your build, you must visit the Witch in the swamp. By paying moola to have your "fortune read," you unlock Card Slots. You can have up to three active cards at Tier 3.

**The "High Noon" Meta Build:**
By combining specific cards like **High Noon**, **Evil Eye**, and **Folk's High Noon**, players can achieve a "legal aimbot" effect. When Ocular Prowess (X) is active, your shots will slightly curve toward targets, making it nearly impossible to miss during a fast-paced shootout.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I lower my character's age in Western Bridger?**

A: You must find and consume the **Dog's Bane Herb**. This item is most commonly found as a rare catch while fishing or by exploring the deep forest biomes. Reducing your age is important for maintaining peak physical stats.

**Q: Where is the best place to level up fast?**

A: For a safe experience, the Fishing Village is unbeatable. However, if you are confident in your combat skills, participating in random world events and bandit outpost raids provides faster experience at a higher risk.

**Q: What is the maximum Tier I can reach?**

A: Currently, the maximum level is Tier 3. Reaching this tier allows you to equip three different Ability Cards simultaneously, which is necessary for endgame PvP builds.

**Q: Can I store my items so I don't lose them on death?**

A: Yes, you can visit the Bank in Ridge County. Storing your excess moola and rare crafting materials there is highly recommended to prevent losing progress during a surprise ambush.
