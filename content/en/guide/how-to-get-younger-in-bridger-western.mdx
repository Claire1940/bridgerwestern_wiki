## The Ultimate Guide on How to Get Younger in Bridger Western

Once you have reached the hut, you will encounter the Witch NPC. Interact with her to open the dialogue menu. She offers services that deal with the soul and the physical body, but they always come at the price of a Dogbane Herb.

To proceed with the rejuvenation, select the dialogue option: **"Restore my youth."**

### How the Restoration Works
When you choose to restore your youth, the Witch consumes one Dogbane Herb from your inventory. This process is not a total reset to age 18; instead, it reduces your current age by a set increment.

| Service | Cost | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| **Restore Youth** | 1x Dogbane Herb | **-20 Years** from current age |
| **Reset Stand** | 1x Dogbane Herb | Removes current Stand power |

As of the 2026 updates, mastering **how to get younger in bridger western** is essential if you find yourself approaching the age of 80. By using the herb, you can effectively stay in your 20s or 30s indefinitely, provided you are willing to spend time fishing for the necessary ingredients.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Be careful when clicking dialogue options. If you accidentally click "Reset my stand," you will lose your current stand power immediately, and this cannot be undone without finding another corpse part.
