## Character Aging and Life Resets

One of the more punishing mechanics in Bridger Western is the aging system. Every time your character dies in combat or due to environmental hazards, your age increases. As you age, your character's physical appearance changes, and reaching the upper limits of age can have detrimental effects on your performance.

To manage this, you must track the reset of your "youth" using specific herbs. The most vital resource for this is **Dog's Bane**, a rare herb that allows you to reduce your age and reset your character's physical state.

### How Aging Works
1.  **Death Penalty:** Each death adds a year to your character's age.
2.  **Herb Harvesting:** Dog's Bane can be found through fishing or rare world spawns.
3.  **Consumption:** Using the herb immediately subtracts years from your total age, effectively "resetting" your lifespan.

## The Witch's Fortune and Card Rerolls

As you level up (or "Tier Up"), you unlock the ability to equip Passive Cards. These cards provide essential buffs, such as increased reload speed, better accuracy, or even the dreaded "Aimbot" build when combined correctly. However, you are at the mercy of the Witch's RNG when unlocking these.

The Witch is located in a secluded hut within the swamp biome. To reach her quickly, use the "Horse Tech" by holding Shift to sprint and Space to gallop across the water.

### Unlocking Your Build
*   **Tier 1:** Your first card slot is free to unlock.
*   **Tier 2 & 3:** Subsequent slots require a payment of 50 Moola per "Fortune."
*   **Resetting Cards:** If you receive a card that doesn't fit your build, you must "Reject this Fate" and pay again to reroll.

> **💡 Tip:** To achieve the "Aimbot" effect with Ocular Prowess, aim for a card combination involving **High Noon** and **Evil Eye**. This allows you to hit targets even if your crosshair isn't perfectly centered.

## Ridge County: Shop and Horse Resets

Ridge County serves as the central hub for all legal (and some illegal) transactions. Unlike the dynamic world spawns, the shops here are always stocked, but the variety of items like horses can be rerolled to find better stats.

| Service | Location | Cost / Requirement |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Horse Stable** | Front of Train Station | 1,000+ Moola for good stats |
| **Gun Shop** | Ridge County Center | Varies by Weapon |
| **Bank** | Main Street | Free Storage |
| **Witch's Hut** | Swamp Biome | 50 Moola per Reroll |

When buying a horse, do not settle for the first one offered. If the price is below 1,000 Moola, the stats (speed and stamina) are likely subpar. You can reset the horse selection by talking to the NPC again until a higher-priced, high-stat horse appears. ## Advanced Combat Mechanics: Ocular Prowess

While waiting for the **bridger western reset time** on your next Corpse Part, you should master the Ocular Prowess (X key) mechanic. This ability highlights enemies and increases your reload speed. When paired with the correct cards, it becomes the most powerful tool in your arsenal.

### Key Controls to Master
*   **Double Tap W:** Sprint and auto-vault obstacles.
*   **Q:** Roll to dodge incoming fire.
*   **G:** Melee attack (useful if an enemy gets too close).
*   **F:** Emergency stop while on a horse.
*   **X:** Activate Ocular Prowess for tactical advantage.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the exact bridger western reset time for server-wide events?**

A: Most server-wide events, such as the train arrival or major bandit spawns, occur on a 10-to-20-minute cycle. However, individual items like Corpse Parts reset as quickly as every 30 seconds depending on the number of players currently active in the server.

**Q: Can I reset my Stand if I don't like it?**

A: Yes, you can reset your Stand by using a specific reset item or by finding a new Stand Arrow/Corpse Part. Keep in mind that replacing a Stand will permanently delete the previous one, so choose wisely before committing to a new roll.

**Q: How do I reset my character's age if I can't find Dog's Bane?**

A: If you are struggling to find Dog's Bane through fishing, your best bet is to trade with other players or check the Outlaw Hideout. Some NPCs occasionally offer rare herbs for a high price, but fishing remains the most reliable "free" method to obtain them.

**Q: Does the train follow a specific schedule?**

A: The train follows a set loop around the map, stopping at major stations like Ridge County and the Forest Biome. If you are lost, simply find the tracks and wait; the train will eventually arrive, providing a safe way to travel between biomes.
