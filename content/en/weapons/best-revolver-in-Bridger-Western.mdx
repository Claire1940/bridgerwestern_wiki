## FAQ

**Q: What is the best revolver in Bridger Western for beginners?**

A: For pure beginner comfort, Taurus .357 is the easiest starting point because it is accessible and forgiving. If you can afford an upgrade and aim with discipline, Whitney Dragoon becomes the stronger long-term pick.

**Q: Is Whitney Dragoon better than Schofield 6 in PvP?**

A: In many duels, yes—especially when headshots are involved. Dragoon tends to reward precision more, while Schofield can feel smoother for players who prefer steady follow-up shots.

**Q: Should I use fan fire or single shots with revolvers?**

A: Default to single, controlled shots at medium range. Use fan fire mostly in closer, high-pressure moments where tracking is harder and burst output matters more than perfect precision.

**Q: What primary weapon pairs best with the best revolver in Bridger Western?**

A: A close-range primary like the Maverick 88 is a strong pairing. It covers rush fights, while your revolver handles medium-range finishing and cleaner target picks.
