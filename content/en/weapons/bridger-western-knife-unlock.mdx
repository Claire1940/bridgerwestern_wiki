## Melee Combat Mechanics and Controls

Once you have completed your **bridger western knife unlock**, you need to know how to use it effectively. Equipping a Saber or similar heavy melee weapon often prevents you from using primary firearms, meaning you must commit to a "Swordmaster" playstyle. 

The controls for melee are straightforward but require precision:
*   **Left Click (M1):** Basic swing/slash.
*   **Right Click (M2):** Block or parry (depending on your equipped cards).
*   **Q:** Roll/Dodge (essential for closing the gap against gun users).
*   **X:** Ocular Pro (highlights enemies and can slow down time with specific cards).

## FAQ

**Q: Where exactly is the bridger western knife unlock vendor?**

A: The primary vendor for the Saber (the main melee weapon) is located in the Ridge County Gun Shop. If you are looking for specialized melee tools like Steel Wires, you must visit the hidden vendor in the Outlaw Hideout near the swamp.

**Q: Can I use a gun and a knife at the same time?**

A: While you can use the "G" key to perform a quick melee strike with a gun equipped, purchasing a dedicated melee weapon like the Saber usually occupies your primary slot, preventing the use of large rifles. However, some smaller pistols may still be usable depending on your specific loadout.

**Q: Is the "Show Me a Good Time" card required for melee?**

A: It is highly recommended. Without it, you cannot parry bullets, making it extremely difficult to approach skilled players who are using high-accuracy weapons like the Whitney Dragon or Winchester.

**Q: How do I stop aging in Bridger Western?**

A: Every time you die, your character ages. To reduce your age, you need to find or catch "Dog's Bane" herbs or specific rare fish that can be traded or consumed to reset your age stats.

**Q: What is the best horse for a melee build?**

A: Look for a horse that costs at least 1,100 Moola in Ridge County. Higher-priced horses have better stamina and speed, which is crucial for chasing down ranged players or escaping a bad encounter after a melee skirmish.
