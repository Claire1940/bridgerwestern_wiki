## Repairing Your Rusted Relic

Once you've successfully fished up the Rusty Mares Leg, the next crucial step is to find someone capable of restoring it to its former glory. This isn't a task for just any blacksmith or general store owner. There's a specific individual in Bridger Western who specializes in such antique repairs, though his services come at a price.

To initiate the repair process, you'll need to locate this unique NPC. He resides just across the bridge from Ridge County. This location is a common landmark, making him relatively easy to find once you know where to look. Simply travel from Ridge County, cross the main bridge, and you'll spot him. Ensure you have the Rusty Mares Leg in your inventory when you approach him.

The repair itself is straightforward but requires a substantial investment of in-game currency. The repairman charges **1,500 Moola** for his services. This amount can be a significant sum for new players, so it's advisable to save up or engage in other money-making activities before attempting the repair. Once you pay, the repairman will take your rusty firearm, and it will be moved to storage, ready for retrieval.

After the repairman works his magic, your now-functional Mares Leg won't immediately reappear in your inventory. To retrieve your newly repaired Mares Leg, you'll need to make a trip to the bank. The bank is typically found in the main "City Colony" or similar central town area.
