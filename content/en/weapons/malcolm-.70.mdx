## Advanced Combat Tactics

Equipping the **malcolm .70** requires a change in mindset compared to standard gunplay. You cannot afford to engage in "spray and pray" tactics. Instead, follow these professional strategies to improve your efficiency:

### The "Stamina Trap"
By utilizing the **Long Shot** card, your goal is to land the first hit as early as possible. Once the opponent's stamina is drained, they cannot sprint to cover or use defensive Stand abilities. This makes them a "sitting duck" for your second shot or a follow-up from a teammate.

### Jump-Shotting with Wings Man
The **Wings Man** card increases your damage while you are in the air. While the recoil of the **malcolm .70** is high, jumping out from behind cover and firing at the apex of your leap can catch opponents off guard and provide a significant damage boost that can bypass standard armor values.

### Managing the Reload
The "Veteran" card is often debated in the community. While it prevents you from losing a full clip during a partial reload, it isn't always necessary for this specific weapon due to its limited capacity. Instead, focus on using "Secret Technique" to increase your movement speed when health is low, allowing you to retreat and reload safely.

> **💡 Tip:** Always carry at least three tonics. The "Tonic Master" card can increase the healing efficiency, ensuring you stay in the fight long enough to cycle through your shots.

## FAQ

**Q: Is the Malcolm .70 good for beginner players?**

A: It is generally considered a high-skill weapon. Beginners may find the slow fire rate and high recoil punishing. It is recommended to practice your aim with the Winchester before moving up to the **malcolm .70**.

**Q: Does the "Executioner" card work with this weapon?**

A: Yes, though it recently received a nerf. You now need to deal 40 damage to trigger a decapitation instead of 20. Since this weapon regularly deals 60+ damage, it can still reliably trigger the effect on wounded enemies.

**Q: Which Stand pairs best with a Malcolm .70 build?**

A: Stands with "Binding" or "Stun" abilities are ideal. Anything that keeps the target stationary for one to two seconds will allow you to line up a guaranteed headshot with the **malcolm .70**, which is usually an instant kill.

**Q: How do I counter a player using this weapon?**

A: High mobility is the best counter. Use cards like "Bergist" to go invisible during rolls or "Freeunner" to slide and dodge. If they miss their shot, you have a 2-3 second window to rush them before they can fire again.
