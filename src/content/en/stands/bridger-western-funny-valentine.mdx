## The Man With Infinite Spin: Final Steps

The final stage of the **bridger western funny valentine** questline shifts from exploration to high-intensity combat. You are required to find the final piece of the puzzle and defeat a legendary foe to prove your mastery over the dimensional powers of D4C.

### The Saint's Skull and the Diavolo Showdown
The Saint's Skull is the rarest of the three corpse parts mentioned in this guide. Once obtained and used, your quest log will update to the final objective: **Defeat Diavolo**.

Diavolo is a formidable boss known for his "Time Erasure" ability. When fighting him for the Valentine quest, it is recommended to use a Stand with high defensive utility or long-range projectiles to avoid his devastating counter-attacks.

### Completing the Cycle
After Diavolo falls, the quest is not automatically completed. You must return to Funny Valentine at his location near the basketball court. Speaking to him one last time will trigger the transformation sequence, evolving your D4C into **D4C Love Train**.

A: You can find Funny Valentine behind the **7X2S statue**, which is located near the Basketball Court in the main hub area of *A Universal Time*.
