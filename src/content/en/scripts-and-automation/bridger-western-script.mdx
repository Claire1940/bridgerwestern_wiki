## Optimizing Corpse Part Spawns

Corpse parts are the high-stakes method of getting a Stand. These items spawn randomly across the map, and their spawn rate is heavily influenced by the number of players currently in the server. This creates a dilemma: more players mean a higher spawn chance, but also more competition and a higher likelihood of getting killed before you can claim the prize.

### Server Hopping Strategies

To maximize your efficiency, many veteran players use a "low server" strategy. By joining a server with the minimum required players (usually at least three), you reduce the competition significantly. 

1.  Navigate to the Bridger: WESTERN page on Roblox.
2.  Scroll down to the "Servers" tab.
3.  Change the "Number of Players" filter to **Ascending**.
4.  Join a server with 2 or 3 players.

While the spawn rate is lower in these instances, your "win rate" for actually securing the corpse part is much higher because you may only be fighting one or two other people.

| Server Type | Spawn Frequency | Competition Level | Recommended Strategy |

| **Medium (6-12 players)** | Occasional | Moderate | Duo with a friend |

## The "Flood" Method: A Community Strategy

One of the most effective ways to get Stands in 2026 without using an external **bridger western script** is the "Flood Method." This requires a coordinated group of players, usually via a Discord community.

The process involves:
1.  Gathering a group of 15-20 players.
2.  Finding a near-empty server.
3.  All players joining at once to "flood" the server.
4.  Taking turns claiming the corpse parts that spawn.

Since everyone in the server is part of the same group, the "competition" is removed, and the high player count ensures that corpse parts spawn at the maximum possible frequency. This is widely considered the best way to get top-tier Stands like Tusk or Star Platinum without relying on luck or cheats.
